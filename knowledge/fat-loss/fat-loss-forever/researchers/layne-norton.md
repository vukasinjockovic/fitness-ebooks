# Layne Norton, PhD
**Type:** researcher
**Status:** solid
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-03
**Aliases:** Dr. Layne Norton, BioLayne

## Summary

Layne Norton is a professional natural bodybuilder (IFPA & NGA Pro), powerlifter, and nutrition scientist with a PhD in Nutritional Sciences from the University of Illinois. His doctoral research focused on protein metabolism and muscle protein synthesis, particularly the role of leucine. He is known for evidence-based approaches to nutrition and training, challenging fitness industry dogma with scientific rigor. Co-author of Fat Loss Forever with Peter Baker.
