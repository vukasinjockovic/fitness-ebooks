# The Bodybuilder's Meal Prep Cookbook
**Author:** Erin Stern
**Type:** Cookbook / Nutrition Guide
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-04

## Overview

A practical cookbook designed for bodybuilders and fitness enthusiasts that combines 64 make-ahead recipes with 8 structured meal prep plans. The book bridges the gap between nutrition theory and practical meal preparation, offering goal-based meal plans for bulking, maintenance, and leaning down.

## Key Value Proposition

Unlike pure theory books, this cookbook provides complete weekly meal prep systems with shopping lists, equipment lists, step-by-step prep instructions, and recipes with full macro breakdowns. Each chapter represents a complete 5-day meal plan aligned to specific physique goals.

## Core Concepts Covered

- **Macronutrients**: Protein as fixed macro; carbs and fats in inverse relationship
- **Energy Balance**: Maintenance calories = weight × activity multiplier (12-14)
- **The 10% Rule**: Adjust calories by ~10% when progress stalls
- **The 90/10 Rule**: 90% on-plan eating, 10% flexibility for life
- **SMART Goals**: Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound
- **Meal Timing**: Pre/post workout meals are most important
- **Carb Cycling**: Higher carb days aligned with heavy training
