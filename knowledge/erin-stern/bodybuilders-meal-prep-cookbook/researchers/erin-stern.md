# Erin Stern
**Type:** researcher
**Status:** solid
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-04
**Aliases:** Erin Stern IFBB Pro, Erin Stern Figure Olympia

## Summary

Erin Stern is a 2x Figure Olympia champion and IFBB Bikini Pro with a background in collegiate track and field. She transitioned from elite athletics to competitive bodybuilding after narrowly missing the Olympic qualifying standard for high jump, bringing an athlete's approach to physique development.

## Background

**Athletic Career:**
- University of Florida athlete (track and field)
- Competed in high jump, pentathlon, heptathlon
- Junior All-American in high jump
- All-SEC Academic honors
- Missed Olympic high jump qualifying by 3cm (jumped 5'11"/1.80m)

**Bodybuilding Career:**
- Won first show (overall figure title) after just 4 weeks prep
- NPC Nationals Overall Figure Champion → immediate IFBB Pro card
- 11 IFBB Pro League titles
- 2x Figure Olympia winner (peak of figure division)
- Returned to competition in IFBB Bikini division (2019)
- Won Pro card in IFBB Bikini at Vietnam Muscle Contest
- Top-15 placing at 2021 Bikini Olympia
