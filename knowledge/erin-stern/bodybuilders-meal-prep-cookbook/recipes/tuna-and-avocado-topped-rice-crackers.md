# Tuna- And Avocado-Topped Rice Crackers

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 6 servings |
| **Serving Size** | about 1/3 cup tuna mixture plus about 14 crackers |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | none |

> These craveable bites make for an extra satisfying snack that offers up plenty of heart-healthy omega-3 fats. Since rice cakes and crackers come in all shapes and sizes, choose your favorite brand and measure them out according to the target macros for this recipe.

## Ingredients

- 12oz (340g) water-packed tuna, drained
- 1 medium avocado
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 1/2 cup chopped celery
- Mini rice cakes or rice crackers (22g carbs per serving) (about 14 pieces)
- Pickled jalapeños, for serving

## Directions

1. Place the tuna, avocado, lemon juice, and celery in a medium bowl. Mix well to combine.
2. Divide the mixture into 6 meal prep containers and store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days. When ready to serve, scoop on top of small rice cakes or rice crackers and then top with chopped or whole pickled jalapeños.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 221 | Total fat 7g | Total carbohydrate 22g | Protein 17g
