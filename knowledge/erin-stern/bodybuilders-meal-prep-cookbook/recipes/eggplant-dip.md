# Eggplant Dip

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 3 cups |
| **Prep Time** | 15 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 25 minutes |

> What most dips are missing is a hefty dose of protein to keep you satisfied. This flavorful dip has the spice and texture you crave, plus the protein you need to power through your workouts.

## Ingredients

- 2 large eggplants, peeled and diced
- 2 tsp olive oil
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp kosher salt, divided
- 2 1/2 tbsp tahini
- 1/4 cup plain nonfat Greek yogurt
- 2 cups low-fat 1% cottage cheese
- Juice and zest of 1 medium lemon
- 1/2 tsp ground cumin
- 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
- 24 pita chips or crackers, for serving

## Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a sheet pan with parchment paper.
2. Place the eggplant on the prepared sheet pan, drizzle with the olive oil, and season with the black pepper and 1/2 teaspoon of the salt. Roast for 20 to 25 minutes or until tender. Remove from the oven and allow to cool slightly.
3. Transfer the eggplant to a food processor. Add the tahini, Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, lemon juice, lemon zest, cumin, smoked paprika, and remaining 1/2 teaspoon salt. Pulse until smooth.
4. Transfer to a large storage container and store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days. Serve with pita chips or crackers.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 207 | Total fat 7g | Total carbohydrate 23g | Protein 15g
