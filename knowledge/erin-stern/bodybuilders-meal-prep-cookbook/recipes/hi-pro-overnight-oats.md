# Hi-Pro Overnight Oats

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings |
| **Serving Size** | 1 jar (about 16oz/473g) topped with 2 tablespoons berries and 1 tablespoon chopped nuts |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes, plus 12 hours to refrigerate |
| **Cook Time** | none |

> This nourishing breakfast never gets boring. There are endless flavor combinations for overnight oats, and the nutrition profile can’t be beat.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups rolled oats
- 4 cups plain, unsweetened almond milk
- 4 tbsp chia seeds
- 4 scoops (25–30 grams each) chocolate protein powder
- 1/2 cup chopped strawberries
- 4 tbsp chopped nuts (almonds, walnuts, or pistachios)

## Directions

1. In a large bowl, combine the oats, almond milk, chia seeds, and protein powder. Mix well to combine.
2. Transfer the oats to 4 small jars with lids. Seal and store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days. When ready to serve, top each serving with 2 tablespoons berries and 1 tablespoon chopped nuts.

## Tips

- Protein powders vary in protein amounts, so make sure you use enough to get 25 to 30 grams per serving.
- Change it up: Try this recipe with other flavors of protein powder or flavor additions like cinnamon, vanilla extract, or cardamom.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 402 | Total fat 12g | Total carbohydrate 40g | Protein 36g
