# Dragon Fruit Smoothie Bowl

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 1 serving |
| **Serving Size** | 1 smoothie bowl |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | none |

> This thick, creamy, high-protein smoothie bowl is a refreshing and satisfying breakfast. It’s also packed with inflammation-fighting antioxidants to fight post-workout inflammation.

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups frozen dragon fruit
- 1 scoop (25–30 grams) vanilla protein powder
- 1/4 cup plain nonfat Greek yogurt
- 1/4 cup coconut water
- 4 tbsp chopped nuts (such as almonds or pistachios)
- 1/4 cup fresh berries (blueberries, blackberries, or raspberries)

## Directions

1. Combine the dragon fruit, protein powder, yogurt, and coconut water in a blender. Blend until smooth.
2. Top with the chopped nuts and berries. Serve immediately.

## Tips

- You can combine all the ingredients except the coconut water in freezer-safe bags. Store in the freezer for up to 3 months.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 479 | Total fat 15g | Total carbohydrate 45g | Protein 42g
