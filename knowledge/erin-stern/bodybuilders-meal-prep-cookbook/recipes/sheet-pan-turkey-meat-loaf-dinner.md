# Sheet Pan Turkey Meat Loaf Dinner

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings |
| **Serving Size** | 1 meat loaf, |
| **Prep Time** | 15 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 30 minutes |

> There’s nothing better than one pan in the oven for a complete, high-protein meal. Make extra of this meat loaf for sandwiches the next day.

## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 lb (566g) ground turkey breast
- 1 egg, beaten
- 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
- 2 tsp Italian seasoning
- 1 1/2 tsp kosher salt, divided
- 1/4 cup no-sugar-added BBQ sauce (such as Noble Made)
- 8 cups cauliflower florets
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 4 cups cooked couscous or Arborio or other short-grain rice, for serving

## Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a sheet pan with parchment paper.
2. In a large bowl, combine the ground turkey, egg, panko breadcrumbs, Italian seasoning, and 1 teaspoon of the salt. With clean hands, mix the ingredients well and then shape into 4 equal-size meat loaves. Transfer to one side of sheet pan and then brush with the barbecue sauce.
3. Place the cauliflower on the other side of sheet pan. Drizzle with the olive oil and then season with the remaining 1/2 teaspoon of salt.
4. Roast in the oven until the internal temperature of the meat loaves reaches 165°F (75°C) and the cauliflower is tender and slightly golden, about 25 to 30 minutes.
5. To each of 4 meal prep containers, add 1 cup of the cooked couscous, 1 meat loaf, and equal amounts of the cauliflower. Store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

## Tips

- Change it up: Don’t have panko breadcrumbs? Use quick-cooking oats instead.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 526 | Total fat 10g | Total carbohydrate 58g | Protein 48g
