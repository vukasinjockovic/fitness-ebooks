# Coconut Yogurt Parfaits

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 parfaits |
| **Serving Size** | 1 parfait |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | none |

> Tasty and beautiful layers of silky yogurt, creamy nut butter, and tangy berries come together for a make-ahead breakfast you will wake up excited to eat. Give the plant-based yogurt a protein boost with a paleo-friendly protein powder.

## Ingredients

- 3 cups coconut yogurt (such as Siggi’s)
- 3 scoops (25–30 grams each) Paleo-friendly protein powder
- 2 tbsp honey
- 1/4 cup creamy almond butter
- 4 cups fresh berries (blueberries, blackberries, or raspberries)

## Directions

1. Combine the yogurt, protein powder, and honey in a medium bowl. Whisk to combine.
2. Into 4 small resealable jars, layer the yogurt mixture, almond butter, and the berries. Cover and store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

## Tips

- Change it up: If you make this with nonfat Greek yogurt, the protein powder isn’t needed. If you want to add some crunch, add chopped almonds or a sprinkle of chia seeds in place of the almond butter.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 434 | Total fat 17g | Total carbohydrate 40g | Protein 34g
