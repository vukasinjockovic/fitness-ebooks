# Marinated Steak Tips

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings |
| **Serving Size** | 3 ounces (85g) steak, 1 cup couscous, and 1 cup asparagus |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes plus 1 hour marinating time |
| **Cook Time** | 25 minutes |

> This is an easy way to prepare a steak dinner, and the bite-size pieces are ideal for separating into meal prep containers. Marinate the steak the morning of your meal prep day or the night before for the best flavor.

## Ingredients

- 2 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- Juice of one medium lemon
- 2 tsp olive oil
- 1 tsp dried dill or oregano
- 1 lb (454g) sirloin steak tips
- 4 cups chopped thin asparagus (see Prep Tip )
- 4 cups cooked couscous

## Directions

1. Combine the Dijon mustard, salt, lemon juice, olive oil, and dried herbs in a medium bowl, and mix well. Add the steak and toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour or up to overnight.
2. Preheat a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add half of the steak to the skillet and sear on all sides; transfer the seared steak to a plate and repeat with remaining steak. Place all the steak tips back into the pan and deglaze with a splash of water or broth. Continue cooking until the steak reaches the desired doneness. Remove from the pan and allow to cool.
3. To each of 4 meal prep containers, add 3 ounces (85g) steak, 1 cup couscous, and 1 cup asparagus. Store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

## Tips

- Thin stalks of asparagus are very quick cooking. Add the chopped pieces to the meal prep containers uncooked and they will steam upon reheating.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 388 | Total fat 10g | Total carbohydrate 42g | Protein 33g
