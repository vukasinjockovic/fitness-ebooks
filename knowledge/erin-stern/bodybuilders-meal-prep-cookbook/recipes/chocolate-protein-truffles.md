# Chocolate Protein Truffles

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 10 truffles |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | none |

> These delightful, bite-sized protein bombs feature mini chocolate chips and are rolled in coconut flakes. They taste like candy!

## Ingredients

- 1 cup rolled oats
- 5–6 scoops (25–30 grams each) chocolate protein powder (enough for about 15 grams of protein per truffle)
- 3 tbsp creamy peanut butter
- 2 tbsp mini chocolate chips
- Dried coconut flakes
- 18oz (510g) low-fat, low-sugar yogurt (plain or flavored)

## Directions

1. To a large bowl, add the oats, protein powder, peanut butter, and mini chocolate chips. Stir to combine and then roll into balls.
2. Pour the coconut flakes out onto a plate. Roll the truffles in the coconut flakes to coat.
3. For high-carb servings, serve with 6 ounces (170g) yogurt on the side. Store the truffles in a large airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

## Tips

- Change it up: If you want to avoid adding additional calories, roll the truffles in cocoa powder instead of coconut flakes.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 156 | Total fat 6g | Total carbohydrate 11g | Protein 16g
