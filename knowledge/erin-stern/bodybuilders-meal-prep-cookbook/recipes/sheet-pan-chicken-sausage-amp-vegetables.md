# Sheet Pan Chicken Sausage & Vegetables

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings |
| **Serving Size** | 2 cups sausage and vegetable mixture and |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 20 minutes |

> This recipe is a great way to use up whatever is left in the vegetable drawer or pantry—or you can choose on-sale veggies. Use lean chicken sausage to keep the fat macros in check while on this plan.

## Ingredients

- 1 lb (454g) chicken sausage, sliced
- 1 small red onion, sliced
- 1 small head cauliflower, chopped into florets
- 1 bunch asparagus, chopped
- 1 medium red bell pepper, chopped
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 3 cups cooked grain-free pasta (such as Jovial)

## Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a sheet pan with parchment paper.
2. Place the chicken sausage and vegetables on the sheet pan. Drizzle with the olive oil, season with salt and black pepper, and toss gently. Cook for 20 to 25 minutes or until the vegetables are slightly tender and the chicken sausage reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C).
3. Remove from the oven and allow to cool completely. Add 2 cups of the sausage and vegetable mixture along with 3/4 cup of the cooked pasta to each of 4 meal prep containers. Store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

## Tips

- Change it up: Experiment with different types of vegetables and seasoning blends: curry powder, chili powder, or herbs de Provence can all add lovely flavor to this basic recipe.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 438 | Total fat 19g | Total carbohydrate 42g | Protein 32g
