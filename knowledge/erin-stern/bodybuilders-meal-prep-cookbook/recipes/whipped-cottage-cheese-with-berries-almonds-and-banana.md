# Whipped Cottage Cheese With Berries, Almonds, And Banana

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 5 servings |
| **Serving Size** | 1 |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | none |

> Give cottage cheese a whole new identity by whirling it into a creamy, pudding-like consistency. Fresh fruit and crunchy almonds make this almost better than an ice cream sundae!

## Ingredients

- 7 cups low-fat 1% cottage cheese
- 4 tbsp honey
- 2 cups fresh berries
- 10 tbsp sliced almonds
- 5 medium bananas, sliced

## Directions

1. Place the cottage cheese and honey in a food processor or blender. Blend until creamy and then transfer to a large storage container.
2. When ready to serve, top with berries, almonds, and sliced bananas. Store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 467 | Total fat 10g | Total carbohydrate 56g | Protein 43g
