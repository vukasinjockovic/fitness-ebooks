# Grilled Chicken With Sautéed Zucchini

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings |
| **Serving Size** | 5oz (142g) cooked chicken, 2 cups zucchini, and 1 |
| **Prep Time** | 15 minutes, plus 1 hour to marinate chicken |
| **Cook Time** | 20 minutes |

> This dish captures the fresh flavors of summer and provides plenty of healthy protein, perfectly-portioned carbs, and antioxidants to help you meet all your weekly goals.

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 lb (680g) skinless, boneless chicken breast
- 2 tsp olive oil
- 3 medium zucchini, diced into large chunks
- 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
- 6 cups cooked whole-grain pasta, for serving

**For the marinade**
- Juice of 1 medium lemon
- 2 tsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp chopped fresh rosemary
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes

## Directions

1. In a large zipper-lock bag, prepare the marinade by combining the lemon juice, olive oil, rosemary, garlic, salt, and red pepper flakes. Seal and rotate gently to combine. Add the chicken to the bag, seal, and rotate again to coat the chicken in the marinade. Transfer to the refrigerator to marinate for 1 hour or up to 24 hours.
2. Remove the chicken from the refrigerator and discard the marinade. Preheat a grill or grill pan to medium high. Grill the chicken for 6 to 7 minutes per side or until internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C).
3. While the chicken is grilling, heat the oil in a large nonstick skillet. Add the zucchini and tomatoes, and season with the salt. Sauté until zucchini is slightly tender.
4. Transfer the grilled chicken and cooked vegetable mixture to 4 meal prep containers along with the pasta, top with Parmesan. Store in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 572 | Total fat 11g | Total carbohydrate 67g | Protein 56g
