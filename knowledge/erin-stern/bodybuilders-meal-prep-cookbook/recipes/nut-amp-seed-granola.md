# Nut & Seed Granola

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings |
| **Serving Size** | 1 |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 20 minutes |

> This grain-free granola adds the perfect amount of crunch to salads, smoothies, or bowls of yogurt or cottage cheese. It’s lightly sweetened and the perfect pairing with fresh fruit.

## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup raw almonds
- 1/4 cup raw cashews
- 2 tbsp dried coconut
- 2 tbsp chia seeds
- 2 tbsp honey
- 5 cups low-fat 1% cottage cheese, for serving
- 2 cups sliced fresh strawberries, for serving

## Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 300°F (150°C). Line a sheet pan with parchment paper.
2. Combine the almonds, cashews, coconut, chia seeds, and honey in a food processor. Pulse just until chopped.
3. Transfer the mixture to the prepared sheet pan and spread into an even layer. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes, tossing about every 5 minutes, then remove from the oven and allow to cool completely.
4. Place 3 tablespoons of the cooled granola, 1 1/4 cups cottage cheese, and 1/2 cup strawberries into each of 4 sectioned meal prep containers. Store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

## Tips

- You can make a double batch of this granola and store it in the freezer for up to 3 months.
- Change it up: Make with pumpkin seeds for more iron, or add 1/2 to 1 teaspoon of ground spices like cinnamon or cardamom.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 442 | Total fat 13g | Total carbohydrate 43g | Protein 41g
