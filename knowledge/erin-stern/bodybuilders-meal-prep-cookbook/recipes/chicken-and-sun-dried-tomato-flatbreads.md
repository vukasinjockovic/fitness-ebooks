# Chicken And Sun-Dried Tomato Flatbreads

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings |
| **Serving Size** | 1 flatbread |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 20 minutes |

> Love pizza? This version has more protein, less fat, and fewer carbs than regular pizza—it’s nourishing and satisfying, and you’ll still feel like you’re indulging! You can experiment with various toppings as the seasons change.

## Ingredients

- 4 thin-style flatbreads (such as lavash)
- 1/2 cup prepared pesto sauce
- 1/2 cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes
- 16oz (454g) cooked chicken breast, chopped
- 1 1/2 cups part-skim shredded mozzarella cheese

## Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Line 2 sheet pans with parchment paper.
2. Arrange 2 flatbreads on each sheet pan. Top each with equal amounts of pesto, followed by the chicken and sun-dried tomatoes. Sprinkle with the cheese.
3. Place in the oven and bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly, about 15 to 20 minutes.
4. Cut into 4 pieces and place in 4 meal prep containers. Store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days or in the freezer for up to 3 months.

## Tips

- You can make these extra fast in the air fryer, just reduce the cook time to 8 to 10 minutes.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 460 | Total fat 7g | Total carbohydrate 30g | Protein 29g
