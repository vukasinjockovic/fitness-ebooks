# Chicken And Rice Bake

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings |
| **Serving Size** | 1 chicken thigh and |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 45 minutes |

> This one-pan wonder will quickly become one of your favorite meal prep recipes. Place everything in a baking dish and the work is essentially done. Roasting chicken with the bone in and skin on ensures it will remain tender and flavorful, but you should remove the skin before eating to keep your macros on target.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup uncooked long-grain white rice
- 3 cups low-sodium chicken or vegetable broth
- 1 tsp kosher salt, divided
- 1 tsp black pepper, divided
- 4 (6oz/170g) bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 medium red onion, roughly chopped
- 1 medium tomato, roughly chopped
- Chopped fresh cilantro, for serving

## Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Place the rice and broth in a 9×13-inch (22×33cm) baking dish. Season with 1/2 teaspoon salt and 1/2 teaspoon black pepper. Stir to combine.
2. Arrange the chicken thighs on top of the rice and then season with the smoked paprika and remaining salt and black pepper. Add the onion and tomato in an even layer on top of the rice. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 30 minutes.
3. After 30 minutes, remove the foil, increase the oven temperature to 425°F (220°C), and bake for an additional 25 to 30 minutes or until the rice is tender and the internal temperature of the chicken reaches 165°F (75°C). Allow to cool slightly and then sprinkle the chopped cilantro over the top.
4. Once cooled, transfer 1 chicken thigh and 3/4 cup of the rice mixture to each of 4 meal prep containers. Store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

## Tips

- You don’t even need to rinse the dry grains of rice for this recipe, just toss everything in the baking dish.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 356 | Total fat 7g | Total carbohydrate 37g | Protein 34g
