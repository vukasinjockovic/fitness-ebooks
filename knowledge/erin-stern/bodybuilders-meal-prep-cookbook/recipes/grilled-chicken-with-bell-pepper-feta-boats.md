# Grilled Chicken With Bell Pepper Feta Boats

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings (1 lower carb and 3 higher carb) |
| **Prep Time** | 1 hour 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 20 minutes |

> This simple, lower-carb meal is the perfect combination of flavors, textures, and macros! For high-carb days, you’ll be adding some peas and extra rice to boost the carbs.

## Ingredients

- 1lb (454g) boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- 4 red or yellow bell peppers, quartered lengthwise and seeded
- 1/3 cup plus 6 tbsp feta cheese
- 2 2/3 cups cooked Arborio or other short-grain rice
- 2 cups frozen peas

**For the marinade**
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 2 tsp Italian seasoning
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper

## Directions

1. In a large zipper-lock bag, prepare the marinade by combining the lemon juice, Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper. Add the chicken breasts, seal, rotate the bag gently to coat the chicken, and then transfer to the refrigerator to marinate for one hour or up to 24 hours.
2. After the chicken is marinated, heat a grill or grill pan to medium-high. Grill the chicken for 6 to 7 minutes per side or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C).
3. While the chicken is grilling, place the bell peppers cut sides up on a plate and fill each with the feta cheese. Gently transfer the peppers to the grill or grill pan and grill until just warmed through, about 4 to 5 minutes.
4. Cook the peas according to package instructions.
5. Remove the chicken and bell peppers from the grill or grill pan and set aside to cool before transferring to 4 meal prep containers. Add the rice to the containers and then add the peas to the high-carb serving containers. Store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

## Tips

- Use frozen peas, as they are just as nutritious as fresh.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 338 | Total fat 14g | Total carbohydrate 21g | Protein 33g
