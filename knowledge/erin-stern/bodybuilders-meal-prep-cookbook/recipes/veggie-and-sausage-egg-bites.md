# Veggie And Sausage Egg Bites

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 12 egg bites |
| **Serving Size** | 3 egg bites plus 1 English muffin |
| **Prep Time** | 15 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 25 minutes |

> This easy, perfectly portioned protein will make your mornings easier. The combination of eggs, egg whites, and chicken sausage keep the protein high but the fat low. Goat cheese is lower in lactose and thus easier to digest for most people.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup egg whites
- 6 large eggs
- 3/4 tsp kosher salt
- Pinch of black pepper
- 5oz (142g) precooked chicken sausage, diced
- 4 cups baby spinach, roughly chopped
- 1 medium bell pepper, finely chopped
- 4oz (113g) goat cheese, crumbled
- 4 whole-grain English muffins or 4 slices whole-grain bread

## Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Spray a 12-cup large muffin pan with cooking spray.
2. Add the egg whites and eggs to a large bowl and gently whisk, then add diced sausage, chopped spinach, chopped bell pepper, and crumbled goat cheese. Mix gently and thoroughly; season with salt and black pepper.
3. Pour the mixture into the prepared muffin cups, making sure to evenly distribute the ingredients. Bake for 25 minutes or until cooked through.
4. Once cooled, transfer to meal prep containers and store in the refrigerator for up to 1 week. When ready to serve, warm the bites in a microwave or toaster oven, and serve with a whole-grain English muffin or a slice of whole-grain bread on the side.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 441 | Total fat 16g | Total carbohydrate 40g | Protein 33g
