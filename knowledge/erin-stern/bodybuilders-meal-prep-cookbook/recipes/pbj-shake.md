# Pbj Shake

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 1 shake |
| **Serving Size** | 1 shake |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | none |

> The magical combination of peanut butter and peanut butter powder gives this shake big flavor while keeping the fat and calories in check. Experiment with different types of frozen berry blends to see which combo you enjoy most—all berries offer a high dose of cell-protecting antioxidants.

## Ingredients

- 1 medium frozen banana
- 1 tbsp creamy peanut butter
- 1 tbsp peanut butter powder (such as PB2)
- 1/2 cup frozen strawberries
- 1 scoop (25–30 grams) vanilla protein powder
- 1 cup unsweetened almond milk

## Directions

1. In a blender, combine the banana, peanut butter, peanut butter powder, strawberries, protein powder, and almond milk. Blend until smooth. Enjoy immediately.

## Tips

- You can combine all the ingredients except the liquids in freezer-safe bags. Store in the freezer for up to 3 months.
- Change it up: This also tastes amazing with chocolate or salted caramel protein powder.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 438 | Total fat 14g | Total carbohydrate 42g | Protein 40g
