# Taco Casserole

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings (1 lower carb and 3 higher carb) |
| **Prep Time** | 20 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 20 minutes |

> This one-pan wonder can be a vehicle for any lean protein, cheese, and tortilla combination you can dream up—just layer, bake, and eat! This also freezes well, so make an extra batch for the freezer if you have time.

## Ingredients

- 1 small yellow onion, chopped
- 1 large red bell pepper, chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 lb (454g) lean ground beef
- 2 tsp taco seasoning
- 8 medium corn tortillas
- 3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 4oz (113g) plain nonfat Greek yogurt
- 3/4 cup black bean salsa (such as Newman’s Own or Trader Joe’s)
- 3 medium apples

## Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Preheat a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add the onion and bell pepper, and cook for 2 to 3 minutes or until slightly soft. Add the garlic, ground beef, and taco seasoning. Cook until the beef is browned, draining off any excess liquid if needed.
3. Spray the bottom of a 9×13–inch (22×33cm) glass baking dish with cooking spray. Arrange half the tortillas along the bottom of the dish. Layer in half the beef mixture, followed by half of the cheese. Repeat with the remaining tortillas, beef mixture, and cheese.
4. Cover with foil and bake for 10 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
5. Cut into 4 equal-size portions and then transfer to 4 meal prep containers. For higher-carb days, serve topped with Greek yogurt and salsa, along with an apple on the side; for lower-carb days, serve with the Greek yogurt sauce. Store the casserole in the refrigerator for up to 5 days or freeze for up to 3 months.

## Tips

- Change it up: You can make your own taco seasoning by combining equal amounts of chili powder, cumin, onion powder, and celery salt.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 341 | Total fat 14g | Total carbohydrate 20g | Protein 34g
