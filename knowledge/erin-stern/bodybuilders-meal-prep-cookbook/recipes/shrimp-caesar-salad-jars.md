# Shrimp Caesar Salad Jars

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings |
| **Serving Size** | 1 (16fl oz/473ml) jar |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | none |

> Assembling salads in jars is a meal prep dream, and combining protein-rich quinoa, lean shrimp, and fresh veggies balances the macros perfectly. Keeping the ingredients layered assures freshness for days.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup no-sugar-added Caesar dressing (such as Primal Kitchen)
- 1 lb (454g) cooked large shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 3 cups cooked quinoa
- 8 cups chopped romaine lettuce

## Directions

1. To each of 4 16-ounce (473ml) jars, add 2 tablespoons dressing, 1/4 pound shrimp, 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese, 3/4 cup quinoa, and 2 cups lettuce.
2. Cover and store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days. To serve, shake well and then pour into a bowl.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 417 | Total fat 13g | Total carbohydrate 39g | Protein 37g
