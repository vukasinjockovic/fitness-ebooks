# Slow Cooker Chicken Chili

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings |
| **Serving Size** | 1 |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 4 hours on high or 8 hours on low |

> This set-it-and-forget-it meal is perfect for any time of year! It’s a low-fat, one-pot combo of lean protein and healthy carbs that is also packed with fiber to help keep you feeling satisfied for hours. This recipe also freezes beautifully, so make a double batch if you have time.

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 1/2 lb (680g) ground chicken breast
- 1–2 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 1 small white onion, chopped
- 1 medium green bell pepper, chopped
- 1 (15oz/425g) can kidney beans, rinsed and drained
- 1 (28oz/794g) can crushed tomatoes
- 2 cups cooked Arborio or other short-grain rice, for serving

## Directions

1. Set the slow cooker to sauté mode. Add the oil. (If your slow cooker doesn’t have a sauté mode, you can do this in a skillet and then transfer the mixture to the slow cooker.)
2. When the oil is hot, add the chicken, chili powder, paprika, and salt. (Add 1 tablespoon chili powder if you prefer your chili less spicy, add 2 tablespoons if you prefer your chili be more spicy.) Cook for 3 to 4 minutes and then add the onion, bell pepper, kidney beans, and tomatoes. Stir, cover, and cook for 4 hours on high or 8 hours on low .
3. Allow to cool and then transfer to 4 meal prep containers along with the rice. Store in the refrigerator for up to 1 week or in the freezer for up to 3 months.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 520 | Total fat 13g | Total carbohydrate 52g | Protein 49g
