# Air-Fryer Chicken Fajitas

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings |
| **Serving Size** | 5oz (142g) cooked chicken, 2 cups cauliflower rice, and 2 tortillas |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 20 minutes |

> One-pan, quick-cooking, paleo-friendly, and you’re welcome. If you don’t have an air fryer, just cook these in a skillet until the chicken is cooked through—about 25 minutes.

## Ingredients

- 1lb (454g) boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into thin strips
- 1 large yellow onion, sliced
- 3 medium bell peppers, sliced
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp chili powder
- 3/4 tsp kosher salt
- 8 cups cooked cauliflower rice
- 8 grain-free tortillas (such as Siete brand)

## Directions

1. Combine the chicken, onions, and peppers in a large bowl. Drizzle with the olive oil and then season with the chili powder and salt. Toss well to coat everything in the spices.
2. Pour the chicken mixture into the basket of an air fryer and cook at 375°F (190°C) for 18 to 20 minutes or until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C). Stop the air fryer periodically to toss the chicken mixture with tongs.
3. Remove the mixture from the basket and allow to cool completely. Divide between 4 meal prep containers and add 2 cups cauliflower rice and 2 tortillas to each container. Store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 435 | Total fat 18g | Total carbohydrate 37g | Protein 36g
