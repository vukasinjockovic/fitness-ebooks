# Energy Bites

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 12 bites |
| **Serving Size** | 2 bites |
| **Prep Time** | 40 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | none |

> Energy bites are a glorious handheld snack—these bites boast plenty of protein and fiber to help fight hunger in between meals. Store in the refrigerator or the freezer for a grab-and-go goodie.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup vanilla protein powder
- 1 cup rolled oats
- 1 tsp ground cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 cup cashew butter
- 1/3 cup honey
- 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips

## Directions

1. Combine the protein powder, oats, cinnamon, and salt in a medium bowl. Mix to combine.
2. Add the cashew butter, honey, and chocolate chips. Mix until a sticky dough is formed. Place the mixture in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
3. Roll the chilled mixture into 12 evenly sized balls and place them in a large airtight container. Store in the refrigerator for up to 1 week or in the freezer for up to 1 month.

## Tips

- Change it up: You can use white chocolate instead of chocolate chips, or add extra flavor with some lemon or orange zest.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 440 | Total fat 12g | Total carbohydrate 46g | Protein 26g
