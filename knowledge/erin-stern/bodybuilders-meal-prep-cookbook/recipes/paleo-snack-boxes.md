# Paleo Snack Boxes

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 6 servings |
| **Serving Size** | 2 hard-boiled eggs and 1 cup grapes |
| **Prep Time** | 25 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | none |

> This simple combination creates the perfect macro balance for the paleo plan. Extra-large eggs are the best choice for the macros.

## Ingredients

- 12 extra-large eggs
- 6 cups red or green grapes

## Directions

1. Place the eggs in a large pot and fill the pot with enough cold water to cover the eggs. Bring to a boil over high heat. Once the water is boiling, turn off the heat, cover, and allow the eggs to sit for 15 minutes before transferring to an ice bath to cool.
2. To each of 6 meal prep containers, add 2 hard-boiled eggs and 1 cup grapes. Cover and store in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 228 | Total fat 11g | Total carbohydrate 17g | Protein 15g
