# Double Protein Blueberry Muffins

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 8 muffins |
| **Serving Size** | 1 muffin |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 22 to 25 minutes |

> Pass on those sugary bakery-style muffins! These muffins will actually help you meet your macro goals. They also freeze very well—to defrost, simply place them in the microwave or toaster oven for a few minutes.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/2 cup vanilla protein powder
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 1 cup low-fat 1% cottage cheese
- 2 large eggs
- 1/3 cup maple syrup
- Zest of 1 lemon
- 1/2 cup fresh blueberries

## Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a 12-cup muffin pan with 8 muffin pan liners.
2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, protein powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
3. In a blender, combine the cottage cheese, eggs, and maple syrup. Blend until smooth.
4. Add the cottage cheese mixture to the dry ingredients and then mix gently to combine. Fold in the lemon zest and blueberries.
5. Scoop 1/2 cup of the batter into each of the prepared cups. Bake for 22 to 25 minutes or until the muffins are puffed and golden and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
6. Remove the muffins from the oven and allow to cool. Store in the refrigerator for up to 1 week or in the freezer for up to 1 month.

## Tips

- Change it up: There are so many delicious flavor combinations you can use for these muffins: try orange zest with raspberries or ground cinnamon with diced apple.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 336 | Total fat 11g | Total carbohydrate 26g | Protein 34g
