# Pizza-Stuffed Zucchini

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings |
| **Serving Size** | 1 zucchini and |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 30 minutes |

> Give summer squash some pizza flavor with high-protein lean beef and a tangy tomato sauce. Serve with grain-free pasta to keep it Paleo-friendly, which also keeps the carbs low.

## Ingredients

- 4 tbsp olive oil, divided
- 4 large zucchini
- 1/2 cup chopped yellow onion
- 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
- 1lb (454g) 95%-lean ground beef
- 2 tsp dried oregano
- Kosher salt
- Black pepper
- 1 (14.5oz/411g) can diced tomatoes
- 3 cups cooked grain-free pasta (such as Jovial)

## Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (205°C).
2. Cut the zucchinis lengthwise and scoop out some of the flesh. Set the boats and scooped flesh aside.
3. Place the zucchini boats cut sides up into a 9×13-inch (23×33cm) glass baking dish. Drizzle with 3 tablespoons olive oil and then season lightly with salt and black pepper. Place in the oven to bake for 15 minutes.
4. While the zucchini is baking, heat the remaining 1 tablespoon olive oil in a medium nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add the beef, reserved zucchini, onion, and garlic. Sauté for 5 minutes.
5. Stir in the diced tomatoes, sprinkle in the oregano, and then season lightly with salt and black pepper.
6. Remove the zucchini from the oven and fill each boat with the beef mixture. Return the zucchini to the oven and bake for an additional 15 minutes or until the zucchini is tender.
7. Remove from the oven and allow to cool completely. Transfer to 4 meal prep containers with the cooked pasta. Store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 442 | Total fat 20g | Total carbohydrate 38g | Protein 29g
