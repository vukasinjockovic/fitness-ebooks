# Paleo Waffles

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 8 waffles |
| **Serving Size** | 2 waffles, 1 tablespoon maple syrup, and |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 25 minutes |

> These scrumptious, grain-free waffles are easy to prepare, and the leftovers freeze beautifully. To up your calories and macros, you can add additional fresh fruit or drizzle with almond butter and sprinkle with cacao nibs.

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups almond flour
- 3 scoops (25–30 grams each) Paleo-friendly protein powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 cup egg whites
- 1 cup plain unsweetened almond milk
- 3 cups fresh berries (blueberries, blackberries, or raspberries)
- 4 tbsp maple syrup, for serving

## Directions

1. Preheat a waffle iron to medium-high. Spray the waffle iron with cooking spray.
2. In a large bowl, combine the almond flour, protein powder, salt, and baking powder. Stir to combine. Add the egg whites and almond milk. Mix until a batter forms.
3. Scoop 1/2 cup of the batter into the waffle iron and cook until golden. Repeat with remaining batter. Set aside to cool.
4. Transfer 2 waffles to each of 4 meal prep containers. Place the berries and maple syrup in small side containers. When ready to serve, top the waffles with the berries and warm syrup. Store in the fridge for up to 5 days.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 477 | Total fat 23g | Total carbohydrate 42g | Protein 33g
