# Spicy Roasted Chicken Thighs With Sweet Potatoes

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings |
| **Serving Size** | 5 ounces (142g) cooked chicken, 1 cup sweet potato, and |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 30 to 35 minutes |

> A fiery spice mix makes these chicken thighs super flavorful but keeps the calories low. And they’re roasted with a healthy carb (sweet potatoes) and broccoli for easy sheet pan meal prep.

## Ingredients

- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1/2 tsp cayenne
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 1/2 tsp kosher salt, divided
- 1 1/2 lb (680g) boneless, skinless chicken thighs
- 4 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
- 3 cups broccoli
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 tsp black pepper

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line 2 large sheet pans with parchment paper.
2. Combine the cumin, smoked paprika, chili powder, cayenne pepper, oregano, and 1 teaspoon salt in a large bowl. Mix to combine. Add the chicken thighs to the bowl and toss to coat in the spice mixture. Place the chicken thighs in a single layer on one of the prepared sheet pans.
3. On the second sheet pan, arrange the sweet potatoes and broccoli, drizzle with olive oil, and then season with the remaining salt and black pepper.
4. Bake until the sweet potatoes are tender and the chicken thighs reach an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C), about 30 to 35 minutes. (Check the oven periodically as the sweet potatoes and broccoli will likely be done sooner.)
5. Once cooked, remove both pans from the oven to cool completely. Transfer 5 ounces (142g) of the chicken, 1 cup sweet potato, and 3/4 cup broccoli to each of 4 meal prep containers. Store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 420 | Total fat 10g | Total carbohydrate 46g | Protein 38g
