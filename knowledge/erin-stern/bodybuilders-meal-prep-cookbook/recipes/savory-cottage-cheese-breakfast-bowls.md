# Savory Cottage Cheese Breakfast Bowls

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings |
| **Serving Size** | 1 cup cottage cheese, |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | none |

> This savory spin on cottage cheese is the perfect way to start your day. Cottage cheese is one of the best sources of leucine—the primary amino acid responsible for building muscle.

## Ingredients

- 1 pint cherry tomatoes, quartered
- 1 small seedless cucumber, chopped
- 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
- 1/4 cup chopped fresh chives
- Juice and zest of 1/2 medium lemon
- 4 cups low-fat 1% cottage cheese, divided
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1 cup chickpea snacks (such as Biena), divided

## Directions

1. In a small bowl, combine the tomatoes, cucumber, parsley, chives, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Gently mix to combine.
2. Add 1 cup of cottage cheese to each of 4 meal prep containers. Season each serving with a sprinkle of salt and black pepper, then top with 3/4 cup of the tomato mixture and 1/4 cup of the chickpea snacks. Store in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 439 | Total fat 14g | Total carbohydrate 40g | Protein 45g
