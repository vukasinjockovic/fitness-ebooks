# Spicy Tofu & Chicken Rice Bowls

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings |
| **Prep Time** | 25 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 20 minutes |

> This recipe is proof that tofu can be exciting! A shot of chili and the high heat of the oven (or an air fryer) make this plant-based protein irresistible. Serve with chicken, veggies, and rice for the ultimate rice bowl.

## Ingredients

- 1 (14oz/397g) container extra firm tofu, well drained and cubed
- 1 tbsp chili sauce (such as sriracha)
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 3 tsp sesame oil, divided
- 1 tbsp cornstarch
- 6 cups mixed vegetables
- 1 cup frozen shelled edamame (thawed)
- 12oz (340g) cooked chicken breast, shredded
- 4 cups cooked Arborio or other short-grain rice

## Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a sheet pan with parchment paper.
2. Wrap the tofu in a clean dish towel and weight it down with a small pot. Set aside to drain for 15 to 20 minutes. Once drained, dice the tofu.
3. In a large bowl, toss the tofu with the chili sauce, soy sauce, 1 teaspoon of the sesame oil, and the cornstarch. Transfer to the prepared sheet pan. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes or until golden and crispy.
4. Heat the remaining 2 teaspoons sesame oil in a large skillet or wok. Add the mixed vegetables, edamame, and chicken, and cook for 5 to 6 minutes or until heated through. (Season with additional chili sauce, if desired.)
5. Divide the chicken mixture across 4 meal prep containers. Add 1 cup rice and 1/4 of the tofu to each container. Once cooled, store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

## Tips

- You can also cook the cubed tofu in an air fryer. To do so, set it to 400°F (205°C) for 10 to 12 minutes.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 504 | Total fat 16g | Total carbohydrate 46g | Protein 46g
