# Buffalo Chicken Salad Sandwiches

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings |
| **Serving Size** | 1 sandwich (1 |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | none |

> A batch of this tangy, high-protein salad will perk up your prep week! It’s an excellent mix of protein and carbs and perfect for a post workout meal. You can dial down the carbs by serving the salad in lettuce cups or on top of mixed greens instead of on bread.

## Ingredients

- 1 (16oz/454g) package shredded cooked chicken breast, drained
- 2 cups chopped celery
- 1/2 cup chopped red onion
- 1/2 cup buffalo sauce
- 1/3 cup crumbled blue cheese
- 8 slices whole-grain bread
- Mixed greens

## Directions

1. In a large bowl, combine the chicken, celery, and onion. Toss to combine.
2. Add the buffalo sauce and blue cheese. Toss again until all ingredients are well combined.
3. Scoop the salad into 4 sectioned meal prep containers, keeping the bread and chicken salad separate until ready to serve. When ready to serve, scoop the salad onto a slice of whole-grain bread. Top with mixed greens, then top with a second slice of bread. Store the salad, greens, and bread separately in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

## Tips

- To make prep easier, you can use leftover, canned, or rotisserie chicken.
- Change it up: This is also delicious with canned tuna or leftover cooked salmon, but it will change the macros slightly.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 577 | Total fat 14g | Total carbohydrate 64g | Protein 50g
