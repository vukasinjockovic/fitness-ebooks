# Greek Yogurt Bagels With Cottage Cheese Eggs

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings |
| **Serving Size** | 1 bagel and 1 cup eggs |
| **Prep Time** | 15 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 20 minutes |

> This spin on homemade bagels and eggs is shockingly good! The bagels are less dense than traditional bagels and pack in more protein since they are made with Greek yogurt. Team them up with cottage cheese-spiked scrambled eggs to round out a protein-rich breakfast.

## Ingredients


**For the bagels**
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 1 cup plain lowfat Greek yogurt
- 1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)

**For the eggs**
- 4 egg whites
- 4 eggs
- 3 cups low-fat 1% cottage cheese
- 4 cups baby spinach
- Salt and black pepper

## Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (205°C). Line a sheet pan with parchment paper.
2. To make the bagels, add the flour, baking powder, salt, yogurt, and egg to a large bowl. Stir until a messy dough forms. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead until the flour comes together. Divide the dough into fourths and roll each piece into a ball. Press to gently flatten and then press a hole in the center using your fingers or the back of a wooden spoon.
3. Place the bagels on the prepared sheet pan and brush with the egg wash. Bake for 23 to 25 minutes or until golden brown.
4. While the bagels are baking, make the eggs by combining the egg whites, eggs, and cottage cheese in a large bowl. Lightly season with salt and black pepper and then whisk to combine. Mix in the baby spinach.
5. Spray a large nonstick skillet with cooking spray and preheat over medium-high heat. Add the eggs and scramble gently until fluffy. Set aside to cool.
6. Remove the bagels from the oven and allow to cool.
7. Add 1 bagel and 1 cup of the egg mixture to each of 4 meal prep containers. Store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days. (The bagels can be stored in the freezer for up to 3 months.)

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 384 | Total fat 8g | Total carbohydrate 38g | Protein 38g
