# Cauli Fried Rice

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings |
| **Serving Size** | 2 cups cauliflower rice, 3 ounces (85g) cooked chicken, and |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 15 minutes |

> This low-carb version of the classic high-calorie takeout favorite is fresher, healthier, and … dare I say … tastier! A balance of veggies, lean protein, and a little crunch hit all the macro targets and is perfect for meal prep.

## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 2 large eggs
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 1 tsp grated fresh ginger
- 8 cups riced cauliflower
- 1 cup chopped or shredded carrots
- 1/2 medium yellow onion, sliced
- 2 cups baby spinach
- 2 tbsp teriyaki sauce
- 12oz (340g) cooked chicken breast, shredded
- 1 cup crunchy chow mein noodles

## Directions

1. Heat the sesame oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the eggs and scramble. Transfer to a plate and set aside.
2. Add the garlic and ginger to the same skillet. Sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.
3. Add the cauliflower, carrots, onion, spinach, and teriyaki sauce. Cook, tossing frequently, for 5 minutes or until the veggies are warmed. Add the cooked egg and chicken and toss to combine.
4. Transfer to 4 meal prep containers and top each portion with 1/4 cup chow mein noodles. Store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

## Tips

- Change it up: You can add some rice and cashews to increase the carbs and healthy fats in this recipe.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 404 | Total fat 12g | Total carbohydrate 38g | Protein 38g
