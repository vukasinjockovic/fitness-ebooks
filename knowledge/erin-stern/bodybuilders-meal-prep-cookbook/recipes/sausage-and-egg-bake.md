# Sausage And Egg Bake

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings (1 lower carb and 3 higher carb) |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 25 minutes |

> Make individual servings of this easy egg breakfast in oven-safe glass containers or ramekins. Once they’re cooled, just pop them right into the fridge to enjoy for the week!

## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 8 egg whites
- 10oz (283g) precooked chicken sausage, diced
- 4 cups diced mixed vegetables
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 7 slices whole-grain bread (such as Ezekiel)
- 3 medium apples
- 1 tbsp creamy almond butter

## Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Spray 4 medium oven-safe ramekins with cooking spray and then place the ramekins on a sheet pan.
2. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, egg whites, chicken sausage, vegetables, salt, and black pepper.
3. Divide the mixture evenly into the 4 ramekins. Transfer the sheet pan to the oven and bake for 20 to 25 minutes or until the eggs are set. Remove from the oven and set aside to cool.
4. Once cooled, cover the ramekins with plastic wrap and store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days. Store the bread separately and serve with the sausage and egg bake and 1 apple when ready to eat.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 362 | Total fat 14g | Total carbohydrate 25g | Protein 36g
