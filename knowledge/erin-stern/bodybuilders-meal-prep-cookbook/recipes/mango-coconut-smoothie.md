# Mango Coconut Smoothie

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 1 serving |
| **Serving Size** | 1 smoothie |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | none |

> This sippable breakfast is exactly what you need on a busy morning or for post-workout recovery. It’s refreshing, hydrating, and contains an ideal balance of protein, carbs, and electrolytes.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup frozen mango chunks
- 1 medium frozen banana
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- 1 scoop (25–30 grams) vanilla protein powder
- 1 cup water
- 1 cup unsweetened coconut milk

## Directions

1. Place the mango, banana, chia seeds, protein powder, water, and coconut milk in a blender. Blend until smooth. Serve promptly.

## Tips

- You can combine all the ingredients except the liquids in freezer-safe bags and store in the freezer for up to 3 months.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 509 | Total fat 10g | Total carbohydrate 60g | Protein 49g
