# Greek-Style Turkey Sliders

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings (one lower carb and three higher carb) |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 20 minutes |

> Lean ground turkey gets a big flavor boost from Mediterranean flavors in these simple but tasty sliders. This recipe can also be used for meatballs and meatloaf depending on how you want to prepare it.

## Ingredients

- 1 lb (454g) 93% lean ground turkey
- 1/3 cup chopped olives (black or green)
- 1 cup diced red bell pepper
- 1 egg, beaten
- 2 tsp dried oregano
- 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- 4 cups steamed broccoli
- 1 large iceberg or Bibb lettuce leaf
- 3 whole-wheat burger buns

## Directions

1. In a large bowl, combine the turkey, olives, bell pepper, egg, oregano, breadcrumbs, and salt. Mix well and shape into 4 patties.
2. Preheat a large nonstick skillet over medium heat and spray with cooking spray.
3. Cook the burgers for 7 to 8 minutes per side or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C). Set aside to cool.
4. To each of 4 meal prep containers, add 1 burger, 1 cup broccoli, and 1 lettuce leaf or 1 burger bun. Store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 303 | Total fat 13g | Total carbohydrate 20g | Protein 28g
