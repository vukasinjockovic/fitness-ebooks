# Grilled Pork Chops With Cucumber Salsa

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings |
| **Serving Size** | 5oz (142g) cooked pork, |
| **Prep Time** | 15 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 20 minutes |

> Pork chops are such an underappreciated lean protein option—they’re super affordable and quick cooking. The cucumber salsa freshens up this meal effortlessly. Serve over a bed of nourishing quinoa for a complete meal.

## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 lb (567g) thin boneless pork loin chops
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Pinch of salt
- Pinch of black pepper
- 4 cups cooked quinoa

**For the salsa**
- 1 large cucumber, diced
- 1 tomato, diced
- 1/4 cup diced onion
- 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
- Juice of 1/2 lime
- Salt, to taste
- Black pepper, to taste

## Directions

1. Make the salsa by combining the cucumber, tomato, onion, cilantro, and lime juice in a small bowl. Toss to combine and then season to taste with salt and black pepper. Set aside until you’re ready to serve.
2. Preheat a grill to medium or preheat a grill pan over medium heat. Drizzle both sides of the loin chops with the olive oil and then season lightly with salt and black pepper.
3. Place the pork chops on the hot grill or grill pan. Cook for 4 to 5 minutes per side or until the internal temperature reaches 145°F (65°C).
4. To each of 4 meal prep containers, add 5 ounces (142g) of the pork, a quarter of the salsa, and 1 cup of the cooked quinoa. Store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 430 | Total fat 12g | Total carbohydrate 41g | Protein 39g
