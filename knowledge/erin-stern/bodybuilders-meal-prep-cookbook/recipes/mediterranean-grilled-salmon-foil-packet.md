# Mediterranean Grilled Salmon Foil Packet

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings |
| **Serving Size** | 1 piece salmon, 1 cup cooked quinoa, and 1 cup steamed broccoli |
| **Prep Time** | 5 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 20 minutes |

> The easiest meal prep has minimal clean up—and it doesn’t get much easier than foil packets tossed onto the grill. Experiment with different herbs and spices with this no-fail method.

## Ingredients

- 1 1/4 lb (567g) wild-caught salmon fillet (skin on)
- 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
- 2 handfuls fresh herbs (such as basil, dill, or oregano)
- 1 medium lemon, sliced
- 3/4 t sp kosher salt
- 4 cups cooked quinoa
- 4 cups steamed broccoli

## Directions

1. Preheat the grill to medium high (about 500°F [260°C]). Place the salmon fillet on a large piece of aluminum foil and then top with the olives, fresh herbs, lemon slices, and salt.
2. Gently wrap the foil around the salmon, loosely pinch the edges shut, and place it on the grill. Cook for 17 to 20 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 145°F (65°C). Carefully transfer the packet to a plate and allow it to cool slightly before opening.
3. Divide the salmon into 4 equal-size portions and then transfer to 4 meal prep containers along with equal portions of the cooked quinoa and broccoli. Store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 423 | Total fat 13g | Total carbohydrate 39g | Protein 36g
