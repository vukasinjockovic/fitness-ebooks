# Fluffy Egg And Quinoa Muffins

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 15 muffins |
| **Serving Size** | 3 muffins |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 50 minutes |

> This versatile recipe can either be followed as written, or it can easily be customized according to your preferences and available ingredients. The addition of quinoa to the egg whites increases the amount of protein, making these the perfect protein-packed meal. These muffins puff up quite a bit while baking, so for best results you can divide the batter between two muffin pans or make two batches—using alternate muffin cups will allow for full expansion of the muffins.

## Ingredients

- 5 large eggs
- 6 cups cooked and cooled quinoa
- 5 cups liquid egg whites
- 3 cups washed and chopped broccoli
- 2 cups washed and chopped mushrooms (such as baby bella or crimini)
- 1/2 cup salsa
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper

## Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Spray 8 cups of a large 12-cup muffin pan with cooking spray.
2. Add the eggs to a large bowl and gently whisk. Place the cooked and cooled quinoa into the bowl and then add the egg whites, broccoli, mushrooms, salsa, salt, and black pepper. Mix until well incorporated.
3. Pour the mixture into the prepared muffin cups, making sure to evenly distribute the ingredients. Bake for 25 minutes or until cooked through. Set aside to cool and repeat the process with the remaining egg mixture.
4. Once cool, transfer the muffins to 5 meal prep containers and store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days or in the freezer for up to 3 months.

## Tips

- Make the quinoa ahead of time and keep in the refrigerator for short term use, or freeze for up to 6 months. Thaw the frozen quinoa in the refrigerator for 1 to 2 days before using.
- Change it up: Add goat cheese and sun-dried tomatoes for a zesty flavor profile. Cooked rice works well in this recipe instead of quinoa. Experiment with different veggies like peppers, onions, carrots, or shredded zucchini.

Calories 497 | Total fat 9g | Total carbohydrate 57g | Protein 45g
