# Bean Pasta With Sausage And Cherry Tomatoes

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 15 minutes |

> Precooked chicken sausage is a fabulous way to add fast protein to a meal—just sauté with tomatoes and toss with high-protein pasta for easy meal prep. This three-ingredient recipe uses traditional Italian flavors, but you can mix it up with various flavors and veggie combos.

## Ingredients

- 12oz (340g) bean pasta (such as Banza)
- 2 tsp olive oil
- 18oz (510g) pre-cooked Italian-style chicken sausage (about 6 links), sliced
- 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1/2 cup chopped fresh basil

## Directions

1. Cook the pasta according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
2. Heat the oil in a large skillet. Add the chicken sausage and cook over medium-high heat until the edges begin to crisp. Add the tomatoes and cook for an additional 2 to 3 minutes.
3. Toss with the pasta and fresh basil. Transfer to meal prep containers and store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

## Tips

- Many chicken sausage varieties come in 12oz (340g) packages, but if your favorite brand doesn’t come in this size, you can store the leftovers in the freezer for up to 3 months.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 561 | Total fat 24g | Total carbohydrate 58g | Protein 45g
