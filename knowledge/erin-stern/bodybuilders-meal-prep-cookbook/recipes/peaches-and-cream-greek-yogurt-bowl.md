# Peaches And Cream Greek Yogurt Bowl

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings (one lower carb and three higher carb) |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | none |

> You can infuse flavor into plain Greek yogurt by using just a few pantry staples. Use fresh peaches when they are in season or opt for frozen peaches during any other time of year.

## Ingredients

- 5 1/2 cups nonfat plain Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 2 tsp ground cinnamon
- 4 tbsp chia seeds
- 4 cups diced fresh peaches (about 4 large)
- 5 tbsp sliced almonds

## Directions

1. In a large bowl, whisk together the yogurt, maple syrup, cinnamon, and chia seeds.
2. Transfer the yogurt to 4 sectioned meal prep containers along with the peaches and almonds.
3. When ready to serve, combine the peaches, yogurt, and almonds, and stir to combine. Store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days

## Tips

- If possible, make this in sectioned meal prep containers and then add the juicy peaches to the yogurt only when you are ready to eat.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 289 | Total fat 12g | Total carbohydrate 23g | Protein 26g
