# Baked Chicken Nuggets With Sweet Potatoes

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings |
| **Serving Size** | 5 chicken nuggets, 4 sweet potato wedges, and 1 cup steamed broccoli |
| **Prep Time** | 15 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 20 minutes |

> This recipe is sure to satisfy a craving for crispy, breaded chicken nuggets. A large batch of these is perfect for weekly meal prep, as they can be served with baked sweet potatoes and veggies, but also added on top of salads or to rice bowls.

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups almond flour
- 1lb (454g) ground chicken breast
- 1 large egg, beaten
- 2 tsp Italian seasoning
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- 1/4 t sp black pepper
- 4 small sweet potatoes, cut into wedges
- 4 cups steamed broccoli, to serve

## Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Place the almond flour in a large shallow dish.
2. In a large bowl, combine the ground chicken, egg, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to combine.
3. Shape the mixture into 20 nuggets and then coat on all sides with the almond flour. Transfer the nuggets and sweet potato wedges to a sheet pan and spray with a light coating of cooking spray. Lightly season the wedges with salt and black pepper.
4. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes or until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C) and the wedges are tender.
5. To each of 4 meal prep containers, add 5 nuggets, 4 sweet potato wedges, and 1 cup broccoli. Store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 460 | Total fat 19g | Total carbohydrate 39g | Protein 38g
