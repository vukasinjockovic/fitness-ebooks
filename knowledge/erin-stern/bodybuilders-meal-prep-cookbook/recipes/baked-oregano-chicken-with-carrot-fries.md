# Baked Oregano Chicken With Carrot Fries

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings |
| **Serving Size** | 4 |
| **Prep Time** | 15 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 25 minutes |

> Oregano isn’t only delicious, it has antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties. This quick-and-easy sheet pan meal is ideal for meal prep, and the roasted carrots are the surprising star of the show!

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 lb (680g) boneless, skinless chicken thighs
- 1 tbsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- Juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1lb (454g) carrots, peeled and cut into sticks
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Pinch of salt
- Pinch of black pepper
- 2 cups cooked Arborio or other short-grain rice

**For the dipping sauce**
- 1/2 cup low-fat Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp sriracha (or other favorite chili sauce)
- Juice of 1/2 medium lemon
- 1 tbsp finely chopped fresh herbs (such as basil, parsley, chives, or dill)

## Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C) and line 2 sheet pans with parchment paper.
2. Place the chicken on one sheet pan and season with the oregano, kosher salt, garlic, and lemon juice.
3. Arrange the carrots on other sheet pan and season with the olive oil, salt, and black pepper.
4. Roast for 25 minutes or until the carrots are golden brown and the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C).
5. While the chicken and carrots are cooking, make the dipping sauce for the carrots by combining the Greek yogurt, sriracha, lemon juice, and chopped herbs in a small bowl. Mix well to combine.
6. Remove the chicken and carrots from the oven and allow to cool slightly. Transfer the chicken, carrots, and rice to 4 meal prep containers. Store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

## Tips

- Change it up: Try this method with any seasonal veggies—cauliflower, summer squash, green beans—they all have similar cook times.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 392 | Total fat 11g | Total carbohydrate 35g | Protein 38g
