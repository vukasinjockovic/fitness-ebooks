# Wild Blueberry Lemon Shake

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 1 serving |
| **Serving Size** | 1 (14 fl oz/414ml) shake |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | none |

> Blueberry and lemon make for an inflammation-fighting powerhouse shake that is packed with vitamin C as well as other potent antioxidants known as anthocyanins. Frozen fruit varieties are just as nutritious as their fresh counterparts because they are packed at the peak of freshness and stored at cold temperatures.

## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup frozen wild blueberries
- 1 scoop (25–30 grams) vanilla protein powder
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- Juice and zest of 1/2 lemon
- 1 cup cold water

## Directions

1. Place the blueberries, protein powder, chia seeds, lemon juice, lemon zest, and water in a blender.
2. Blend until smooth. (Add more water if needed to reach the desired consistency.)

## Tips

- You can combine all the ingredients except the water in freezer-safe bags and store in the freezer for up to 3 months.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 184 | Total fat 4g | Total carbohydrate 24g | Protein 18g
