# Chickpea And Quinoa Salad

| | |
|---|---|
| **Yield** | 4 servings |
| **Serving Size** | 2 cups |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | none |

> This simple salad combines plant protein from quinoa and chickpeas along with chicken and plenty of veggies to meet target macros and give you plenty of satisfying fuel for your workouts.

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Juice of 1 medium lemon
- 2 tbsp minced shallot
- 3 cups cooked quinoa
- 1 (15oz/425g) can chickpeas, rinsed and drained
- 1 medium cucumber, peeled and chopped
- 1/2 cup sliced black olives
- 1/2 cup chopped fresh herbs (such as mint, basil, parsley, or chives)
- 12oz (340g) cooked chicken breast (shredded or diced)

## Directions

1. Combine the olive oil, lemon juice, and shallot in a large bowl. Whisk to combine.
2. Add the quinoa, chickpeas, cucumber, olives, fresh herbs, and chicken breast. Toss well to combine.
3. Transfer to 4 meal prep containers. Store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.

## Nutrition (per serving)

Calories 413 | Total fat 13g | Total carbohydrate 42g | Protein 32g
