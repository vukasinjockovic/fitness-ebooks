# Meal Plan 2: Gaining Muscle (Lean Bulk)

## Summary

**Average Macros:** 40P/40C/20F
**Approximate Calories:** 2,000

> Give your muscles the fuel to grow with this gaining muscle prep plan. In order to gain muscle, you’ll need to be in a slight caloric surplus. Ideally, this surplus should be comprised of protein and carbs, as this will keep body fat gain to a minimum.

## Step-by-Step Prep Plan

1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (205°C). Prep the spaghetti squash for Lighter Chicken Cacciatore with Spaghetti Squash and place it on a sheet pan. Set a timer and bake for 30 to 40 minutes or until tender.
2. While the squash is baking, prepare the dough for the Energy Bites in a large bowl. Set a timer for 30 minutes and place the dough in the refrigerator to chill.
3. Prepare the marinade for the Grilled Chicken with Sautéed Zucchini in a large resealable bag. Add the chicken to the bag and place in the refrigerator to marinate for 1 hour.
4. Preheat a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Prepare the chicken thighs and remaining ingredients for the chicken cacciatore. Cook and then set aside to cool.
5. When the timer for the squash expires, remove the squash from the oven and increase the temperature to 425°F (220°C). Set the squash aside to cool for 10 to 15 minutes.
6. Remove the dough for the energy bites from the refrigerator. Roll the dough into balls and then place them in an airtight container in the refrigerator. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
7. Peel the bananas for the Mango Coconut Smoothies . Place the bananas in a freezer-safe bag and store in the freezer. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
8. Once the spaghetti squash has cooled slightly, use a fork to shred the squash into “spaghetti.” Transfer the squash to meal prep containers and top with the chicken and sauce for the chicken cacciatore. Allow to cool completely. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
9. When the marinating time for the grilled chicken is complete, remove the chicken from the refrigerator and discard the marinade. Preheat a grill pan (or preheat a grill) over medium-high heat. Grill the chicken breasts for 6 to 7 minutes per side.
10. While the chicken breasts are cooking, fill a medium pan with water and place over medium heat. When the water is boiling, cook the pasta for the grilled chicken with zucchini. Drain the pasta and set it aside to cool.
11. When the grilled chicken breasts are done cooking, set them aside to cool. Heat the oil for the zucchini and tomatoes in a large skillet. Add the zucchini and tomatoes and cook until the zucchini is tender and the tomatoes are softened. Set aside to cool. Once cooled, transfer the ingredients to meal prep containers. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
12. Rinse the pot used for the pasta and return it to the stovetop over high heat. Add all the ingredients for the Lean Protein Rice Pudding except the protein powder. Bring to a rolling boil and then reduce the heat to low. Set a timer for 15 minutes.
13. While the rice pudding is simmering, prepare the sheet pan and ingredients for the Sheet Pan Turkey Meat Loaf Dinner . Set a timer and bake for 30 minutes.
14. When the timer for the rice pudding expires, remove the pan from the heat and allow the pudding to cool for 10 minutes before adding the protein powder. Stir to combine and then transfer the pudding to meal prep containers along with the berries and almonds. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
15. Make the couscous according to package directions for the turkey meat loaf dinner.
16. When the turkey meat loaves and caulflower are done baking, remove them from the oven to cool. Once cooled, transfer the meat loaves, couscous, and cauliflower to meal prep containers. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
