## Step-by-Step Prep Plan

1. Prepare the quinoa for the week in a medium saucepan. (You will need 10 cups.)
2. Preheat the grill to medium high. Prepare the salmon fillet for the Mediterranean Grilled Salmon Foil Packet and place the packet on the hot grill. Set a timer for 17 minutes.
3. While the salmon is cooking, prep the ingredients for the Chickpea and Quinoa Salad . Combine the ingredients in a large bowl and then transfer to meal prep containers. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
4. When the timer for the salmon expires, check the salmon for doneness. If the internal temperature has reached 145°F (65°C), remove the packet from the grill and place it on a plate to cool slightly.
5. Prep the ingredients for the Shrimp Caesar Salad Jars . Layer the ingredients into the jars as instructed, seal the jars tightly, and place them in the refrigerator. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
6. Preheat the oven to 425 °F (220°C).
7. Cut the broccoli for the grilled salmon goil packet into florets (4 cups) and then steam in the microwave. Place the broccoli into meal prep containers along with the remaining quinoa and the cooled salmon. Once the ingredients have cooled completely, seal the containers and place them in the refrigerator. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
8. Prep the sheet pan and eggplant for the Eggplant Dip . Place the eggplant in the oven and set a timer for 20 minutes.
9. While the eggplant is roasting, prepare the ingredients to make 2 Dragon Fruit Smoothie Bowls . Chop the nuts and rinse the berries, then place each into separate small storage containers and transfer to the refrigerator. Store the other ingredients separately until ready to serve. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
10. When the timer for the eggplant expires, remove the eggplant from the oven and set it aside to cool. Reduce the oven temperature to 300°F (150°C).
11. Prepare the sheet pan for the Nut & Seed Granola . Combine the dry ingredients and honey in a food processor and pulse until chopped. Spread the mixture in an even layer onto the sheet pan. Set a timer for 15 minutes and place the pan in the oven. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes, tossing the mixture every 5 minutes.
12. While the granola is baking, clean out the food processor and add the eggplant and remaining eggplant dip ingredients. Pulse until well combined. Transfer to a large storage container and place in the refrigerator once cooled completely. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
13. When the granola is done baking, remove it from the oven and set aside to cool completely. Once the granola has cooled, prep the strawberries and place them into sectioned meal prep containers, then add the granola and cottage cheese. Cover the containers and transfer to the refrigerator. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
