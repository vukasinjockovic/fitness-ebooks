# Meal Plan 4: Reduced Carb (Maintenance)

## Summary

**Average Macros:** 30P/25C/45F
**Approximate Calories:** 1,500

> This plan is an excellent choice if you feel you do better with a reduced carbohydrate intake. With these macros, you'll feel satiated all day and won't have the crash that can accompany higher-carbohydrate plans. Feel free to move the higher-carb meals to post-workout to allow for better recovery and refueling after the workout.

## Step-by-Step Prep Plan

1. Prepare the rice for the week in medium saucepan or rice cooker. (You will need a total of 2 cups.)
2. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C). Place the salmon for the Roasted Salmon Tacos with Mango Salsa on a parchment paper-lined baking sheet and then season as instructed. Set a timer for 12 minutes and place the salmon in the oven.
3. While the salmon is cooking, prepare the ingredients for the mango salsa. Combine the ingredients in a medium bowl and transfer to a medium storage container.
4. When the timer for the salmon expires, check the salmon for doneness. If the salmon is cooked to 145°F (65°C), remove from the oven and set aside to cool. Reduce the oven temperature to 350 °F (١٧٥°C).
5. Prep the filling ingredients for the Thai Turkey Lettuce Cups . Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the filling ingredients as instructed, then transfer the filling, lettuce cups, peanuts, and scallions to separate medium storage containers. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
6. Prepare the muffin pan and prep the ingredients for the Veggie and Sausage Egg Bites . Combine the ingredients in a large bowl and season as instructed. Pour the egg mixture into the prepared muffin cups. Set a timer for 25 minutes and place the muffins in the oven.
7. Once the salmon has cooled, transfer the salmon, Greek yogurt, and tortillas to separate medium storage containers. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
8. While the egg bites are baking, prep the ingredients for the Tuna and Avocado-Topped Rice Crackers . Combine the ingredients in a medium bowl and then transfer the mixture to a large storage container. Store the rice cakes or crackers and pickled jalapeños separately until ready to serve. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
9. When the timer for the egg bites expires, remove the egg bites from oven and set aside to cool. Once cooled, transfer the bites to a large storage container. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
10. Prep the ingredients for the Cucumber Hoagies . Assemble the hoagies, individually wrap them tightly in parchment paper, then transfer them to a large zipper-lock storage bag . (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
11. Prep the ingredients for 2 PBJ Shakes by peeling 2 bananas, placing them into a large zipper-lock storage bag, and transferring them to the freezer. Store the other ingredients separately until ready to serve. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
