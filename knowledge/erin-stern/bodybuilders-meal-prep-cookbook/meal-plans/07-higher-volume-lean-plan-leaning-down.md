# Meal Plan 7: Higher Volume Lean Plan (Leaning Down)

## Summary

**Average Macros:** 40P/40C/20F
**Approximate Calories:** 1,300

> One of the most challenging aspects of leaning down is feeling hungry all the time. Adding high-fiber, low-calorie, nutrient-dense foods to your diet can offset feelings of hunger without derailing your caloric deficit. The addition of these foods can make a plate or storage container look fuller, which can also denote feelings of satiety.

## Step-by-Step Prep Plan

1. Make the marinade for the Marinated Steak Tips in a medium bowl. Add the steak tips to the marinade, toss to coat, then transfer to the refrigerator. Set a timer 1 hour.
2. Make the couscous for the steak tips in a medium saucepan. (You will need a little more than 4 cups for the week.) Set aside.
3. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and prepare the sheet pans for the Sheet Pan Protein Banana Pancakes . Make the batter for the pancakes in a blender and then pour it into the prepared sheet pans. Set a timer for 15 minutes and place the sheet pans in the oven.
4. When the timer for the steak tips expires, remove the steak tips from the refrigerator, remove the tips from the marinade, and discard the marinade. Preheat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Prepare the steak tips in the skillet as instructed, then set them aside to cool. Once the steak tips are cooled, transfer them to meal prep containers along with the asparagus and couscous. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
5. When the timer for the pancakes expires, check them for doneness by inserting a butter knife into the center. If the butter knife comes out clean, remove the pancakes from the oven and set aside to cool. Once cooled, cut the pancakes and place them into meal prep containers. Add the yogurt and berries, placing the yogurt in smaller containers until ready to serve. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
6. Keep the oven set to 375°F (190°C). Line 2 sheet pans with parchment paper and prep the ingredients for the Spicy Roasted Chicken Thighs with Sweet Potatoes . Add the seasoned chicken to one sheet pan and the sweet potatoes and broccoli to the other sheet pan. Set a timer for 30 minutes and place the sheet pans in the oven.
7. While the chicken and sweet potatoes are baking, prepare the sheet pan and make the dough for the Greek Yogurt Bagels . Shape the bagels and place them on the prepared sheet pan. Brush with the egg wash and set aside.
8. When the timer for the chicken and sweet potatoes expires, check the chicken for doneness. If the chicken has reached an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C) and the potatoes are tender, remove them from the oven and increase the oven temperature to 400°F (205°C). Set the chicken thighs, sweet potatoes, and broccoli aside to cool. Once cooled, transfer to meal prep containers. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
9. Place the bagels in the oven and set a timer for 23 minutes.
10. While the bagels are baking, make the rice for the Stir-Fried Shrimp with Blistered Green Beans in a medium saucepan. (You will need about 3 cups for the week.) Set aside.
11. Place a large nonstick skillet over high heat and prepare the shrimp and green beans as instructed. Set the shrimp and green beans aside to cool. Once cooled, transfer to meal prep containers along with the rice. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
12. When the timer for the bagels expires, check the bagels for doneness. If they’re golden brown, remove them from the oven and set them aside to cool.
13. Prep the ingredients for the cottage cheese eggs. Clean out the large nonstick skillet, spray with cooking spray, and return it to medium-high heat. Cook the eggs as instructed and set aside to cool. Once the eggs and bagels have cooled, transfer them to meal prep containers. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
14. Prep enough ingredients to make 5 Wild Lemon Blueberry Shakes . Store the ingredients separately until ready to serve. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
