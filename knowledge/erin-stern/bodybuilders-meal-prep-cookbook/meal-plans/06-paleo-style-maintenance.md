# Meal Plan 6: Paleo-Style (Maintenance)

## Summary

**Average Macros:** 30P/30C/40F
**Approximate Calories:** 1,600

> If you’re looking for a maintenance meal plan that’s grain- and dairy-free, this is the plan for you. Balanced carbs and fats can help keep blood sugar levels in check, while higher-fiber foods contribute to feelings of satiety. By cutting out grains and dairy, you may also reduce inflammation if you’re sensitive to these foods.

## Step-by-Step Prep Plan

1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C) and prepare the sheet pans to make the Roasted Mustard Salmon with Rosemary Potato Wedges . Prep the ingredients and place the salmon and potatoes onto separate sheet pans. Place only the sheet pan with the potatoes into the oven and set a timer for 20 minutes.
2. While the potatoes are baking, make the hard-boiled eggs for the Paleo Snack Boxes . Fill a large pot with cold water and add the eggs. Bring to a boil over high heat, then turn off the heat, cover the pot, and set a second timer for 15 minutes.
3. While the eggs and potatoes are cooking, heat the frozen spinach for the mustard salmon in the microwave. (You will need 4 cups for the week.) Drain the spinach and set aside. Prepare an ice bath for the hard-boiled eggs.
4. When the timer for the potatoes expires, place the sheet pan with the salmon into the oven. Reset the timer and bake the potatoes and salmon for an additional 20 minutes.
5. When the timer for the eggs expires, carefully transfer the eggs to the ice bath to cool. Once the eggs are cooled, assemble the snack boxes with the eggs and grapes in meal prep containers. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
6. When the timer for the salmon and potatoes expires, reduce the oven temperature to 375°F (190°C) and remove the sheet pans from the oven. Sprinkle the remaining lemon juice over both pans and set them aside to cool. Once the salmon, potatoes, and spinach have cooled, transfer them to meal prep containers. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
7. Prep the ingredients for the Baked Chicken Nuggets with Sweet Potatoes . Place the nuggets and sweet potato wedges on a clean sheet pan. Set a timer for 15 minutes and place the sheet pan in the oven.
8. While the chicken nuggets are baking, prep the ingredients for the Air Fryer Chicken Fajitas . Place the chicken mixture in the air fryer and set a timer for 18 minutes. (Toss the chicken mixture periodically with tongs.)
9. Cook the cauliflower rice for the fajitas and then set aside.
10. Steam the broccoli for the baked chicken nuggets in the microwave. (You will need 4 cups.)
11. When the timer for the chicken nuggets and sweet potato wedges expires, check the chicken for doneness. If the chicken has reached an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C) and the wedges are tender, remove them from the oven and set aside to cool. Once cooled, transfer them to meal prep containers along with the broccoli. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
12. When the timer for the chicken fajitas expires, check the chicken for doneness. If the chicken has reached an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C), remove the fajitas from the air fryer and set aside to cool. Once cooled, transfer to meal prep containers along with the cauliflower rice and tortillas. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
13. Preheat the waffle iron and spray with cooking spray to make the Paleo Waffles . Prepare the batter and make the waffles as instructed. Set the waffles aside to cool. Once cooled, transfer the waffles to meal prep containers and store the berries and maple syrup in separate containers until ready to serve. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
14. Combine the ingredients for the Coconut Yogurt Parfaits . Layer the ingredients into the jars as instructed and then place the jars into the refrigerator. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
