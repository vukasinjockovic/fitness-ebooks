# Meal Plan 1: Gaining Muscle (Bulk)

## Summary

**Average Macros:** 35P/45C/20F
**Approximate Calories:** 2,100–2,200

> The fastest way to gain muscle is with a bulk plan and to include foods that are flavorful. It’s still important to make sure that the overage of calories comes from carbs and proteins, as this can limit the amount of body fat gained. Protein intake can be reduced to allow for more muscle-building carbs, as carbs are protein sparing.

## Step-by-Step Prep Plan

1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (205°C). Cook the quinoa for Fluffy Egg and Quinoa Muffins in a medium saucepan according to package directions. (You’ll need 6 cups for the week.)
2. While the quinoa is cooking, prepare the chicken breasts and vegetables for the Sheet Pan Smoky Chicken with Roasted Veggies . Place the prepared chicken breasts and vegetables on a foil-lined sheet pan. Set a timer for 20 minutes and bake until the internal temperature of the chicken reaches 165°F (75°C).
3. While the quinoa and chicken are cooking, prepare the muffin pan and egg mixture for the quinoa muffins. Set aside. Mix the ingredients to make the batter for the Peanut Butter Protein Waffles in a large bowl. Set aside.
4. When the quinoa is done cooking, remove it from the saucepan and set it aside to cool slightly. Rinse out the saucepan and fill it with fresh water to make the pasta for the Sesame Noodles with Tofu and Stir-Fry Vegetables .
5. When the water is boiling, add the pasta and cook according to package directions. While the pasta is cooking, make the sauce for the stir fry and prepare the tofu and vegetables for the stir fry.
6. When the chicken and veggies are done baking, remove them from the oven and set aside to cool. Reduce the oven temperature to 350°F (175°C). Transfer the chicken and vegetables to meal prep containers to cool completely. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
7. Once the quinoa has cooled slightly, combine the egg mixture and quinoa for the egg and quinoa muffins. Add the mixture to the prepared muffin cups and place in the oven. Set a timer for 25 minutes and bake until cooked through.
8. Preheat the waffle iron and spray with cooking spray. Make the waffles as directed. Top each waffle with 1 tablespoon peanut butter and transfer to meal prep containers. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
9. When the timer for the egg and quinoa muffins expires, remove the muffins from the oven and set aside to cool completely before placing in meal prep containers. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
10. Once the pasta for the stir-fry is done cooking, remove it from the stovetop and drain. Set aside.
11. Preheat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Cook the tofu and then remove from the pan. Add the vegetables and cook until softened. Add the pasta noodles and sauce, toss to combine, then add the tofu back to the skillet or wok. Cook for about 5 minutes and then transfer to meal prep containers to cool completely. (Prep for this recipe is complete.).
12. Prepare the ingredients for the Buffalo Chicken Salad Sandwiches . Combine the chicken, celery, and onion in a large bowl. Mix to combine and then add the buffalo sauce and blue cheese. Mix again and then divide the salad into 4 sectioned meal prep containers. Place in the refrigerator. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
13. Prepare the ingredients to make the Whipped Cottage Cheese with Berries, Almonds, and Banana . Process the cottage cheese and honey in a blender or food processor. Place the mixture in a large container and then transfer to the refrigerator. Place the berries and almonds in separate containers. Slice and add the banana when ready to serve. (Prep for this recipe is complete.)
