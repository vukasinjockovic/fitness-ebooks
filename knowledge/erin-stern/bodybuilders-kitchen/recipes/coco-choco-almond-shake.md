# COCO-CHOCO ALMOND SHAKE

**Category:** Shakes & Desserts
**Source:** The Bodybuilder's Kitchen by Erin Stern (2018)

> This shake is more satisfying than a candy bar! Healthy fats from the almond butter and coconut keep the carbs low, and antioxidant-rich cocoa powder provides an extra boost of energy.

## Quick Info

- **Servings:** 1
- **Serving Size:** 14oz (400g)
- **Prep Time:** 10 minutes
- **Cook Time:** none

## Ingredients

- 1 cup unsweetened vanilla almond milk
- ¼ cup chocolate whey protein powder
- 1 tbsp almond butter
- 1 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
- 2 tbsp unsweetened shredded coconut
- 3 tsp powdered stevia
- 1 tbsp slivered almonds

## Directions

1. Combine the almond milk, protein powder, almond butter, cocoa powder, coconut, and stevia in a blender. Blend on high for 30 seconds.

2. Scrape the sides of the blender with a rubber spatula, and blend on high for 1 additional minute.

3. Transfer to a glass and top with the slivered almonds. Serve immediately.
