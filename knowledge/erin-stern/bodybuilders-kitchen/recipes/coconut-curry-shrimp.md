# COCONUT CURRY SHRIMP

**Category:** Entrées
**Source:** The Bodybuilder's Kitchen by Erin Stern (2018)

> This Indian-inspired one pot wonder is ready in 15 minutes. The shrimp are complemented by smooth coconut and warm curry flavors, and lime awakens the palate and adds touches of acidity and brightness. This ultra low carb option is also low in fat and packed with protein.

## Quick Info

- **Servings:** 4
- **Serving Size:** 4oz (110g)
- **Prep Time:** 10 minutes
- **Cook Time:** 5 minutes

## Ingredients

- 1 14oz (400g) can light coconut milk
- 1 tbsp fresh-squeezed lime juice
- 1 tbsp curry powder
- 2 tsp freshly grated ginger
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp ground black pepper
- 1lb (450g) shrimp, peeled and deveined

## Directions

1. In a medium saucepan, combine the coconut milk, lime juice, curry powder, ginger, salt, and black pepper. Simmer over low heat for 8 to 10 minutes, allowing the mixture to thicken slightly.

2. Add the shrimp to the pan and cook for an additional 3 to 5 minutes, or until the shrimp just turn opaque. (Do not overcook the shrimp, as they can become rubbery.) Serve hot.

## Prep Tips

- Cook fresh shrimp within 1 day of purchasing.
- Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 days.

## Variations

For a little extra heat, add ½ tsp cayenne powder.
