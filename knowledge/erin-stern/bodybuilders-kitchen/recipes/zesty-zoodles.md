# ZESTY ZOODLES

**Category:** Salads & Sides
**Source:** The Bodybuilder's Kitchen by Erin Stern (2018)

> These noodles have the look and texture of traditional pasta, but contain only a fraction of the carbs and calories! The veggie volume will fill you up without throwing off your meal plan, and an inexpensive spiralizer is all you’ll need to make these delectable, low carb noodles.

## Quick Info

- **Servings:** 4
- **Serving Size:** 1 cup
- **Prep Time:** 5 minutes
- **Cook Time:** none

## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp olive oil mayonnaise
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- 2 tbsp large grain mustard
- ½ tsp salt
- ½ tsp ground black pepper
- 4 medium zucchini, washed and ends trimmed

## Directions

1. In a large bowl, make the dressing by combining the mayonnaise, lemon juice, mustard, salt, and black pepper. Mix well.

2. Create the zoodles by running each zucchini through a spiralizer, making sure to discard the seeds. (Alternatively, you can use a julienne peeler or potato peeler to make ribbons.)

3. Add the zoodles to the dressing, and toss thoroughly to ensure the zoodles are fully coated in the dressing. Serve chilled, or at room temperature.

## Prep Tips

- Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 4 days.

## Variations

For a Mediterranean flavor, omit the mustard and add 1 tsp ground oregano and 1 tsp garlic powder to the dressing.
