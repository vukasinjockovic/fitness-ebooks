# The Bodybuilder's Kitchen

**Author:** Erin Stern
**Published:** 2018
**Subtitle:** 100 Muscle-Building, Fat Burning Recipes

## Overview

This cookbook provides 100 macro-friendly recipes designed for bodybuilders and fitness enthusiasts, along with 5 comprehensive 7-day meal plans for different fitness goals. The book emphasizes nutrient timing, macro management, and simple whole-food cooking.

## Contents

### [Nutrition Concepts](concepts/_index.md)
Core bodybuilding nutrition principles including:
- Macronutrient management (protein, carbs, fats)
- Thermic Effect of Food (TEF)
- Nutrient timing (pre/post workout)
- Bulking vs cutting phases
- Ketogenic, carb cycling, and calorie cycling approaches
- Calorie calculation formulas

### [Meal Plans](meal-plans/_index.md)
5 complete 7-day meal plans with shopping lists:
- 7-Day Bulking Meal Plan (15% fat / 50% carbs / 35% protein)
- 7-Day Cutting Meal Plan (20% fat / 40% carbs / 40% protein)
- 7-Day Ketogenic Meal Plan (60% fat / 30% protein / 10% carbs)
- 7-Day Calorie Cycling Meal Plan (normal + low calorie days)
- 7-Day Carb Cycling Meal Plan (normal + low carb days)

### [Recipes](recipes/_index.md)
100 recipes across 5 categories:
- **Breakfasts** (20 recipes) - Egg dishes, pancakes, oatmeal, bars
- **Entrées** (33 recipes) - Chicken, beef, pork, fish, slow cooker
- **Salads & Sides** (22 recipes) - Vegetables, rice, potatoes
- **Snacks & Power Bars** (13 recipes) - Protein bars, quick bites
- **Shakes & Desserts** (12 recipes) - Protein shakes, healthy treats

## Key Takeaways

1. **Nutrition = 80% of results** - Training is critical but nutrition is the key to body composition
2. **Protein in every meal** - Cannot be stored, must consume throughout day
3. **Time your macros** - Complex carbs pre-workout, simple carbs post-workout
4. **5-6 meals/day** - Keep metabolic fire burning consistently
5. **Don't starve yourself** - Never drop below BMR, slight deficits only
6. **Track and adjust** - Use food logs, make small incremental changes

## Recipe Design Philosophy

- Minimal ingredients (available at any grocery store)
- High protein, macro-friendly
- Easy to meal prep and store
- Variations included for different dietary needs
- Storage tips for meal prep
