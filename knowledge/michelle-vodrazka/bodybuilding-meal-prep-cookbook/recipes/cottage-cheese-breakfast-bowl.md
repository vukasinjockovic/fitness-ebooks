# COTTAGE CHEESE BREAKFAST BOWL

**Category:** Prep Week Recipes
**Source:** The Bodybuilding Meal Prep Cookbook by Michelle Vodrazka (2019)
**Tags:** Vegetarian

> This breakfast bowl is great for people who think they don’t like cottage cheese, as yogurt, berries, and nut butter add flavor and texture. To prep, you’ll scoop ingredients straight into their individual containers for an irresistible ready-to-go breakfast.

## Quick Info

- **Servings:** 3
- **Macros:** 40% Protein / 33% Carbs / 27% Fat

## Ingredients

- 2 cups (450g) reduced-fat cottage cheese (2%), divided
- 1 cup (227g) nonfat vanilla Greek yogurt, divided
- 1 cup (100g) fresh blueberries or raspberries, divided
- 3 tablespoons (45g) low-sugar blueberry or mixed berry jam, divided
- 3 tablespoons (22g) toasted pecan pieces or sliced almonds (optional), divided
