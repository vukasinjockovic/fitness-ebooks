# POPEYE’S FAVORITE SPINACH

**Category:** Vegetables and Grains
**Source:** The Bodybuilding Meal Prep Cookbook by Michelle Vodrazka (2019)
**Tags:** Gluten-Free, Vegetarian

> If you’ve never tried creamed spinach, you’re in for a treat. This veggie side dish includes eggs for added protein. Serve with roast beef, pork, chicken, or any other meat for a well-rounded meal.

## Quick Info

- **Servings:** 4
- **Macros:** 23% Protein / 13% Carbs / 64% Fat

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons (30ml) extra-virgin olive oil
- ½ large yellow onion (80g), finely chopped
- 2 (10-ounce) packages (600g) chopped frozen spinach, slightly thawed
- 2 garlic cloves (10g), crushed
- 3 large eggs
- Sea salt
- Freshly ground black pepper

## Directions

1. In a medium saucepan over medium heat, heat the olive oil. Add the onion and sauté, stirring occasionally, for about 2 minutes.

2. Add the spinach and continue to cook uncovered for 2 more minutes, stirring regularly. Cover and continue cooking at a low temperature for about 15 more minutes, until the spinach is fully cooked.

3. Add the garlic and eggs, and stir just until the eggs are fully cooked. Season with salt and pepper. Remove from the heat and let cool.

4. Divide the spinach and eggs evenly among 4 airtight storage containers and seal.
