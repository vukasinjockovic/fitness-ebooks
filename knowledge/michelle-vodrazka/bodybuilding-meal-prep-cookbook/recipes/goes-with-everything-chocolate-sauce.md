# GOES-WITH-EVERYTHING CHOCOLATE SAUCE

**Category:** Staples and Sauces
**Source:** The Bodybuilding Meal Prep Cookbook by Michelle Vodrazka (2019)
**Tags:** Gluten-Free, Vegan

## Quick Info

- **Macros:** 3% Protein / 33% Carbs / 64% Fat

## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon (15ml) coconut oil
- ½ cup (180g) dairy-free mini chocolate chips
- 1 tablespoon (15ml) pure maple syrup
- 1 tablespoon (15ml) unsweetened vanilla almond milk
- Pinch sea salt

## Directions

1. In a small pot over medium-low heat, melt the coconut oil and chocolate chips, stirring constantly so the chocolate doesn’t burn.

2. Once melted, add in the maple syrup, almond milk, and salt and mix until well blended.

3. Remove from the heat and use immediately or transfer to a glass container, cool, and seal.
