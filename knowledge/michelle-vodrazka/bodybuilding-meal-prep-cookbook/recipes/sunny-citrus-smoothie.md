# SUNNY CITRUS SMOOTHIE

**Category:** Prep Week Recipes
**Source:** The Bodybuilding Meal Prep Cookbook by Michelle Vodrazka (2019)
**Tags:** Nut-Free, Vegetarian

> Ready to start your day with a burst of tropical sweetness? This sunny citrus smoothie will hit the spot. High-protein Greek yogurt helps you stay full even with a low calorie count. Or feel free to add a scoop of protein powder to get even more of this muscle-building macro.

## Quick Info

- **Servings:** 5
- **Macros:** 16% Protein / 80% Carbs / 4% Fat

## Ingredients

- 1¼ cups (175g) frozen pineapple chunks, divided
- 1¼ cups (175g) frozen papaya chunks, divided
- 1¼ cups (175g) frozen mango chunks, divided
- 2½ fresh or frozen bananas, chopped (300g), divided
- 2½ tablespoons (17.5g) ground flaxseed, divided
- 2½ cups (275g) nonfat vanilla Greek yogurt, divided
- 2½ cups (625ml) orange juice, divided
