# TUNA BEAN SALAD

**Category:** Prep Week Recipes
**Source:** The Bodybuilding Meal Prep Cookbook by Michelle Vodrazka (2019)
**Tags:** Gluten-Free, Nut-Free

> Sometimes you just need a no-brainer of a meal, am I right? Well, you’re in luck. This tuna bean salad is a true grab-and-go entrée that can be eaten at any time, no microwaving required. A mix of heart-healthy tuna, olive oil, and multiple beans make it not just convenient but extremely healthy, too.

## Quick Info

- **Servings:** 2
- **Macros:** 34% Protein / 53% Carbs / 13% Fat

## Ingredients

- 4 cups (440g) blanched green beans
- 2 cups (450g) mixed cooked beans, rinsed and drained
- 1 cup (164g) frozen corn kernels, thawed
- 1 cup (180g) halved grape tomatoes
- 2 tablespoons (30ml) Dijon mustard
- 2 tablespoons (30ml) red wine vinegar
- Drizzle extra-virgin olive oil or avocado oil
- 1 (6-ounce) (165g) can flaked tuna, drained

## Directions

1. In a medium bowl, mix together the green beans, mixed beans, corn, tomatoes, mustard, and vinegar. Drizzle with the olive oil and mix again.

2. Into each of 2 airtight storage containers, put half a can of tuna and 4 cups of bean salad and seal.
