# PEANUT BUTTER

**Category:** Sweet and Savory Snacks
**Source:** The Bodybuilding Meal Prep Cookbook by Michelle Vodrazka (2019)
**Tags:** Gluten-Free, Vegan

> Who says ice cream needs to contain actual cream? Frozen bananas add natural sweetness, and peanut butter adds richness and creaminess, making this dairy-free version almost too good to be true. High in carbohydrates, protein, and potassium, this nice cream makes a great post-workout recovery snack.

## Quick Info

- **Servings:** 4
- **Macros:** 10% Protein / 55% Carbs / 35% Fat

## Ingredients

- 4 fresh or frozen bananas (480g), chopped, divided
- 4 tablespoons (64g) creamy peanut butter, divided
- 4 pinches ground cinnamon, divided
- 4 to 8 tablespoons (60 to 120ml) almond or coconut milk, divided
- 2 teaspoons (10ml) vanilla extract, divided
