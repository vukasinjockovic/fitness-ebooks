# ORANGE JULIUS SMOOTHIE

**Category:** Breakfast
**Source:** The Bodybuilding Meal Prep Cookbook by Michelle Vodrazka (2019)
**Tags:** Gluten-Free, Vegan

> This smoothie isn’t just orange in flavor—it’s orange in color, too, from both the orange juice and the (surprise!) baby carrots. Just one smoothie serves over 40 percent of your day’s iron and over a quarter of your daily fiber requirement.

## Quick Info

- **Servings:** 2
- **Macros:** 23% Protein / 40% Carbs / 37% Fat

## Ingredients

- 1 cup (90g) frozen mango chunks, divided
- 1 cup (50g) chopped baby carrots, divided
- 1 fresh or frozen banana (118g), chopped, divided
- 1 scoop (30g) vanilla vegan protein powder, divided
- 2 tablespoons (10g) hemp hearts, divided
- 8 to 12 ice cubes, divided
- ½ cup (120ml) orange juice, divided
- ½ cup (120ml) unsweetened almond milk, divided
