# NUT

**Category:** Breakfast
**Source:** The Bodybuilding Meal Prep Cookbook by Michelle Vodrazka (2019)
**Tags:** Gluten-Free, Vegan

> Do you love berries as much as I do? Any kind will work in this versatile breakfast bowl. Experiment with blackberries, strawberries, or marionberries for a pop of juicy goodness on top of the softened oat mixture.

## Quick Info

- **Servings:** 2
- **Macros:** 15% Protein / 23% Carbs / 62% Fat

## Ingredients

- 1 cup (240ml) unsweetened vanilla almond milk
- 2 tablespoons (17g) raw pumpkin seeds
- 2 tablespoons (24g) chia seeds
- 2 tablespoons (28g) hemp hearts
- 2 tablespoons (16g) walnuts
- 2 tablespoons (12g) rolled oats
- 1 tablespoon (16g) unsweetened almond butter
- ⅓ cup (48g) blueberries
- ⅓ cup (40g) raspberries

## Directions

1. In a medium bowl, mix together the almond milk, pumpkin seeds, chia seeds, hemp hearts, walnuts, and oats.

2. Let sit on the counter to thicken for 10 minutes. Stir in the almond butter.

3. Into each of 2 storage containers, place half of the soaked mixture. Top each with half of the blueberries and raspberries and seal.
