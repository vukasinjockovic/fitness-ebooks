# MORNING MOCHA SMOOTHIE

**Category:** Prep Week Recipes
**Source:** The Bodybuilding Meal Prep Cookbook by Michelle Vodrazka (2019)
**Tags:** Gluten-Free, Vegan

> It doesn’t get much easier than smoothies for a grab-and-go breakfast—especially when you’ve made them ahead of time and can pull a smoothie pack right from the freezer. This one is for coffee lovers.

## Quick Info

- **Servings:** 4
- **Macros:** 37% Protein / 46% Carbs / 17% Fat

## Ingredients

- 4 fresh or frozen bananas (472g), chopped, divided
- 16 to 20 ice cubes, divided
- 4 scoops (120g) vanilla vegan protein powder, divided
- 4 tablespoons (28g) unsweetened cocoa powder, divided
- 4 cups (950ml) unsweetened vanilla almond, cashew, or coconut milk, divided
- 1 cup (250ml) brewed coffee, divided
- 4 teaspoons (20ml) vanilla extract, divided
