# EASY GUACAMOLE

**Category:** Staples and Sauces
**Source:** The Bodybuilding Meal Prep Cookbook by Michelle Vodrazka (2019)
**Tags:** Gluten-Free, Nut-Free, Vegan

> After hummus, guacamole is my second favorite dip. I love how it tastes both creamy and refreshing at the same time, and it’s perfect for those of you in the bulking or maintenance phases. I like to serve guacamole as part of a salad or bento box lunch, on top of a Buddha bowl or burrito bowl, or simply as a spread for toast.

## Quick Info

- **Macros:** 5% Protein / 20% Carbs / 75% Fat

## Ingredients

- 2 ripe avocados (300g), halved, pitted, peeled, and mashed
- Juice of 1 large lime (30ml)
- 1 garlic clove (5g), minced
- 2 tablespoons (20g) finely chopped red onion
- 1 tablespoon (4g) finely chopped fresh cilantro
- 20 grape tomatoes (120g), finely chopped (optional)
- ½ teaspoon (3g) sea salt
- Freshly ground black pepper
