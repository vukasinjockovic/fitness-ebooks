# AVOCADO-LIME DRESSING

**Category:** Staples and Sauces
**Source:** The Bodybuilding Meal Prep Cookbook by Michelle Vodrazka (2019)
**Tags:** Gluten-Free, Nut-Free, Vegetarian

> You know the phrase “the icing on the cake” for a special finishing touch? Sometimes I think we should start using the phrase “the dressing on the salad” instead. This vinaigrette makes a great accompaniment to a Mexican salad or any leafy greens. Keep in mind that, as written, this does make a large batch—so feel free to reduce it to suit your needs.

## Quick Info

- **Macros:** 0% Protein / 4% Carbs / 96% Fat

## Ingredients

- 1 large ripe avocado (170g), halved, pitted, and peeled
- Sea salt
- Ground white pepper
- 2 tablespoons (30ml) white wine vinegar
- 1¼ cups (300ml) avocado oil
- ¼ cup (60ml) freshly squeezed lime juice
- Pinch ground cayenne pepper
- 1 tablespoon (15ml) honey
