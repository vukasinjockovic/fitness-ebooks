# DIJON VINAIGRETTE

**Category:** Staples and Sauces
**Source:** The Bodybuilding Meal Prep Cookbook by Michelle Vodrazka (2019)
**Tags:** Gluten-Free, Nut-Free, Vegan

> Everyone needs a good go-to vinaigrette to toss with salads. This one is my personal favorite for both its taste and the antioxidant, immune-boosting, and anticancer properties it provides. Use a high-quality Dijon mustard for the best flavor.

## Quick Info

- **Macros:** 0% Protein / 3% Carbs / 97% Fat

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons (30ml) freshly squeezed lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons (30ml) Dijon mustard
- 6 tablespoons (90ml) extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 garlic clove (5g), minced
- 8 large (20g) basil leaves, minced
- Sea salt
- Freshly ground black pepper
