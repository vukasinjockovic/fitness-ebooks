# MAPLE-TAHINI SAUCE

**Category:** Staples and Sauces
**Source:** The Bodybuilding Meal Prep Cookbook by Michelle Vodrazka (2019)
**Tags:** Gluten-Free, Nut-Free, Vegan

> Tahini is a sesame paste that’s the basis for hummus. Who would guess that it would pair so well with the sweetness of maple syrup? This multipurpose sauce is great to have on hand for a variety of dishes (see tip).

## Quick Info

- **Macros:** 10% Protein / 10% Carbs / 80% Fat

## Ingredients

- ½ cup (256g) tahini
- Juice of 1 lime (30ml)
- 1 tablespoon (15ml) pure maple syrup
- 1 tablespoon (15ml) extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 teaspoon (5ml) Dijon mustard
- ½ cup water
- Sea salt
