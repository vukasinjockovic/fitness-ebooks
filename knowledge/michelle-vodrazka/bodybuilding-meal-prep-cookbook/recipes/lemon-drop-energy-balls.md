# LEMON DROP ENERGY BALLS

**Category:** Sweet and Savory Snacks
**Source:** The Bodybuilding Meal Prep Cookbook by Michelle Vodrazka (2019)
**Tags:** Gluten-Free, Vegan

> These potent little lemony cancer-fighters are one of my favorite treats. They taste just divine and are naturally high in minerals, antioxidants, vitamin C, fiber, and heart-healthy fats, making them the perfect snack for any time of day. I especially love them as a post-workout treat. Pop one—or two, or three.

## Quick Info

- **Macros:** 7% Protein / 46% Carbs / 47% Fat

## Ingredients

- 1¼ cups (310ml) raw cashews
- 10 Medjool dates (240g), pitted
- ½ cup (120ml) shredded, unsweetened coconut, divided
- 1 tablespoon (15ml) coconut oil
- Zest of 1 lemon
- 1 tablespoon (15ml) freshly squeezed lemon juice

## Directions

1. In a food processor, process the cashews until roughly ground so that only tiny pieces remain. Remove from the food processor and set aside.

2. In the food processor, combine the dates, ¼ cup of coconut, and the coconut oil and lemon zest, and process until the dates are mashed.

3. Add the ground cashews and lemon juice, and process until a ball of dough starts to form.

4. Remove the dough from the food processor and, with your hands, roll into 1½-inch balls.

5. In a shallow bowl, place the remaining ¼ cup of coconut and roll the balls in the shredded coconut until well covered.

6. Into each of 6 resealable storage bags (freezer bags if freezing), place 2 energy balls, and seal.
