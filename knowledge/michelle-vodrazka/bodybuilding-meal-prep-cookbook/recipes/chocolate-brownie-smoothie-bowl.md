# CHOCOLATE BROWNIE SMOOTHIE BOWL

**Category:** Breakfast
**Source:** The Bodybuilding Meal Prep Cookbook by Michelle Vodrazka (2019)
**Tags:** Gluten-Free, Vegan

> Now you get to eat chocolate for breakfast and reap the rewards of those green nutritional gems called avocados. With more than 20 vitamins and minerals, avocados are one of the most nutritious foods on the planet. You will never be able to guess they are hiding in this creamy chocolate smoothie.

## Quick Info

- **Servings:** 4
- **Macros:** 11% Protein / 34% Carbs / 55% Fat

## Ingredients

- 4 fresh or frozen bananas (472g), chopped
- 2 avocados (300g), halved, pitted, and peeled
- 2 scoops (60g) chocolate vegan protein powder
- 4 tablespoons (29g) cocoa powder
- 4 tablespoons (24g) instant coffee
- 28 ice cubes
- 2 cups (472ml) unsweetened vanilla almond milk, divided
