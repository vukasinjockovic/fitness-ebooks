## Overview

Thinner Leaner Stronger (TLS) is the women's version of Bigger Leaner Stronger (BLS). While it shares the same core philosophy—heavy compound lifting, progressive overload, proper nutrition—it adapts the program for women with a slightly higher rep range (8-10 vs 4-6) and addresses women-specific fitness myths.

## Key Differentiator from BLS

The TLS Training Formula uses **8-10 reps** instead of BLS's **4-6 reps**. This is the primary modification, with all other principles (compound lifts, progressive overload, calorie management) remaining the same.
