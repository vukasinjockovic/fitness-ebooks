## Summary

Louie Simmons is the founder of Westside Barbell, one of the most successful powerlifting gyms in history. He's credited with popularizing concurrent periodization in Western strength training, adapting methods from Soviet sports science.

## Key Contribution

### Concurrent Periodization

> "Concurrent periodization has you train each biomechanical capacity (rep range) in each workout. This method was developed in Russia and is touted by many strength and bodybuilding experts (such as Louie Simmons of Westside Barbell fame) as the optimal way to periodize your training."
— Ch. 6

The concurrent approach trains all qualities simultaneously rather than focusing on one at a time, preventing detraining while building multiple capacities.

## Westside Barbell Legacy

Westside Barbell has produced numerous world-record powerlifters using Simmons' conjugate/concurrent system. The gym has been influential in spreading Soviet training methods to American lifters.
