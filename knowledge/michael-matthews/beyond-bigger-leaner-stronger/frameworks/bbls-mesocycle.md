# BBLS Mesocycle

**Type:** framework
**Status:** solid
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-04
**Aliases:** Beyond BLS Training Cycle, 6-Week Cycle

## Summary

The training cycle structure for Beyond Bigger Leaner Stronger: a 6-week mesocycle consisting of 4 normal weeks, 1 power week, and 1 deload/rest week. Each week hits all three training modalities (power, myofibrillar, sarcoplasmic) with varying emphasis.

## The 6-Week Structure

| Week | Type | Focus |
|------|------|-------|
| 1-4 | Normal Week | Myofibrillar growth + supplementary power & sarcoplasmic |
| 5 | Power Week | Pure strength + supplementary myofibrillar & sarcoplasmic |
| 6 | Deload/Rest | Recovery |

## Normal Week Workout Structure

For all workouts except shoulders and arms:

1. **Warm-up** - 4 sets building to working weight
2. **Power Sets** - 2 sets × 2-3 reps @ 90%+ 1RM
3. **Myofibrillar Sets** - 6 sets × 4-6 reps @ 80-85% 1RM
4. **Sarcoplasmic Sets** - 2 sets × 8-10 reps @ 70-75% 1RM

**Example Chest Workout:**
- Warm-up on Bench Press
- 2 × Bench Press (Power)
- 3 × Incline Bench Press (Myofibrillar)
- 3 × Incline Dumbbell Press (Myofibrillar)
- 2 × Dips weighted (Sarcoplasmic)

## Power Week Workout Structure

1. **Warm-up** - 4 sets
2. **Power Sets** - 2 sets × 2-3 reps
3. **One-Rep Max Set** - 1 set × 1 rep
4. **Myofibrillar Sets** - 3 sets × 4-6 reps
5. **Sarcoplasmic Sets** - 2 sets × 8-10 reps

**Example Back Workout:**
- Warm-up on Deadlift
- 2 × Deadlift (Power)
- 1 × Deadlift (One-Rep Max)
- 3 × Barbell Row (Myofibrillar)
- 2 × Pull-Ups weighted (Sarcoplasmic)

## Set Type Details

### Power Sets

> "For all workouts but arms, you will start your workouts with Power Sets, which are for pure strength training... working with heavy weights that allow for no more than 2 to 3 reps (90%+ of your 1RM)."
— Ch. 7

- Tempo: 2-1-1 (2 sec down, pause, explosive up)
- Rest: 3-5 minutes between sets
- Duration: 10-15 seconds per set

### Myofibrillar Sets

> "After your power training, you'll move on to sets performed in the 4 to 6 rep range (80% to 85% of your 1RM)."
— Ch. 7

- Tempo: 2-1-1
- Rest: 2-3 minutes between sets
- Duration: 20-25 seconds per set

### Sarcoplasmic Sets

> "Last are your Sarcoplasmic Hypertrophy Sets, which entail working in the 8 to 10 rep range (70% to 75% of your 1RM)."
— Ch. 7

- Tempo: 3-1-2 (slower, more time under tension)
- Rest: 1-2 minutes between sets
- Duration: 30-40 seconds per set

## Weekly Training Split

5-day preferred approach:

| Day | Focus |
|-----|-------|
| Day 1 | Chest & Abs |
| Day 2 | Back & Calves |
| Day 3 | Shoulders |
| Day 4 | Arms & Abs |
| Day 5 | Legs |
| Day 6-7 | Cardio or Rest |

## Deload Options

### Option 1: 5-Day Deload
- 9 sets per workout, compound exercises
- 10 reps × 40% 1RM per set
- 1 minute rest between sets
- "Easy. You never push to failure."

### Option 2: 3-Day Deload
- Push/Pull/Legs split
- 9 sets per workout
- 10 reps × 40% 1RM
- Push: Bench, Military, Close-Grip Bench
- Pull: Deadlift, Row, Pull-Up
- Legs: Squat, Lunge, Leg Press

## Workout Volume

> "Overall, moderate volumes (~30 to 60 repetitions per session for training) appear to yield the largest responses."
— Ch. 7, citing Goteborg University research

BBLS delivers ~60 reps per workout with mixed intensities.
