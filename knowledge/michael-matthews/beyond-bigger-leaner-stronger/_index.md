## Overview

The advanced sequel to Bigger Leaner Stronger for experienced lifters who have completed 1.5-2 years of the BLS program. Introduces periodization, concurrent training across multiple rep ranges, and advanced nutrition strategies for maintaining single-digit body fat.

## Prerequisites

> "If you can do the following, you're ready to start Beyond Bigger Leaner Stronger. If you can't, you should use BLS to get there."

| Lift | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| Squat | 1.75 × body weight for 4-6 reps |
| Deadlift | 1.75 × body weight for 4-6 reps |
| Bench Press | 1.35 × body weight for 4-6 reps |
| Military Press | 1.0 × body weight for 4-6 reps |

## Core Philosophy

BBLS targets three goals for advanced lifters:
1. **Improving proportions and aesthetics** - Using the Grecian Ideal measurements
2. **Improving strength one rep at a time** - Progressive overload remains king
3. **Staying healthy** - Avoiding overtraining, injury, and metabolic damage
