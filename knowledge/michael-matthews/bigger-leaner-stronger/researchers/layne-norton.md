## Summary

Layne Norton is a professional bodybuilder with a PhD in Nutritional Sciences. His research on protein metabolism, particularly leucine's role in muscle protein synthesis, is cited multiple times in BLS. He bridges the gap between academic research and practical bodybuilding application.

## Key Research Cited

### Leucine and Protein Synthesis

> "Layne E. Norton, Gabriel J. Wilson, Donald K. Layman, Christopher J. Moulton, and Peter J. Garlick, 'Leucine Content of Dietary Proteins Is a Determinant of Postprandial Skeletal Muscle Protein Synthesis in Adult Rats,' Nutrition and Metabolism 9, no. 1 (2012): 67."
— Endnotes, Ch. 11

This research supports Matthews' recommendations for high-quality protein sources and the importance of leucine content.
