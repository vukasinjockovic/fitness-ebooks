## Summary

Brad Schoenfeld is a well-known fitness researcher whose work on muscle hypertrophy mechanisms is cited in BLS. His research provides scientific support for heavy compound training principles.

## Key Research Cited

### Mechanisms of Muscle Hypertrophy

Matthews cites Schoenfeld's 2010 paper in the Journal of Strength and Conditioning Research:

> "Brad J. Schoenfeld, 'The Mechanisms of Muscle Hypertrophy and Their Application to Resistance Training,' Journal of Strength and Conditioning Research, 24, no. 10 (2010): 2857-72."
— Endnotes, Ch. 6

This research forms part of the scientific foundation for BLS training principles.

## Matthews' Endorsement

Listed first among the most respected people in the fitness industry:

> "Many of the most respected people in this industry, such as Brad Schoenfeld, Charles Poliquin, Mark Rippetoe, Layne Norton, Alan Aragon, Lyle McDonald, and Pavel Tsatsouline, also advocate the same style of training for maximal results in the gym."
