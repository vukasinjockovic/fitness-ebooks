## Summary

Scott Stevenson, PhD is an exercise scientist and bodybuilder who collaborated with John Meadows to provide the scientific foundation for Mountain Dog Training. In "The Brutality of Mountain Dog Training," he contributes detailed explanations of the physiological mechanisms underlying each training technique, supporting Meadows' experiential knowledge with research citations.

## Role in Book

- Provides exercise science explanations for each technique
- Supplies research citations (200+ references)
- Explains "method to the madness" of Mountain Dog Training
- Bridges gap between practical bodybuilding and scientific literature

## Key Quote from Book

> "If you follow any of the Mountain Dog training videos... you have no doubt seen some of the outright crazy stuff we do. This article is where you learn the method behind the madness (written in John's own words), and why the Mountain Dog Training system, rooted in decades of John's real world ball busting experience, is also well-supported by solid exercise science (supplied by Scott)."
