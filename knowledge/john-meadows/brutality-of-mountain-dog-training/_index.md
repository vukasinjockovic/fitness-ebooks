## Overview

A comprehensive guide to the Mountain Dog Training system, combining John Meadows' decades of practical bodybuilding experience with exercise science explanations from Scott Stevenson, PhD. The book details the 4-phase workout structure, intensification techniques, and periodization approach that defines Mountain Dog Training.

## Core Framework

The book centers on the **4-Phase Workout Structure** for "Base" workouts:

1. **Phase 1: Pre-Pump Activation** - Joint-friendly exercises to pump blood into muscles before heavy work
2. **Phase 2: Explosive Exercise** - Heavy compound movements with bands/chains
3. **Phase 3: Supra-Maximal Pump** - Intensification techniques for metabolic stress
4. **Phase 4: Loaded Stretching** - Full ROM exercises emphasizing stretched position

## Key Themes

- **Injury Prevention Through Structure** - Pre-pump work before heavy lifting extends longevity
- **Instinctive Periodization** - Listen to your body rather than rigid timelines
- **Metabolic Stress as Hypertrophy Driver** - The pump matters for muscle growth
- **Training Intensity Techniques** - Weider principles applied systematically

## Periodization Model

Three-phase macrocycle (~3 months):
1. **Preparation** (2-3 weeks) - Lower volume, high intensity, establish baseline
2. **Destroyer** (5-6 weeks) - Maximum volume and intensity, challenge sets
3. **Taper** (2-3 weeks) - Maintain intensity, reduce volume, realize gains
