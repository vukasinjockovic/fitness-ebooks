## Research Gaps Noted in Book

1. **High-frequency training** - Limited research on advanced trainees
2. **Myo-reps** - No direct comparison studies to standard sets
3. **Forced reps** - No research comparing to drop sets
4. **Cheat reps** - No controlled studies
5. **Volume cycling** - Theoretical support but limited research
6. **Specialization phases** - Anecdotal evidence from natural bodybuilders

## Citation Format Reference

Standard format used in book:
```
Author et al., "Title," Journal Volume, no. Issue (Month Year): Pages, URL.
```

Example:
```
Brad J. Schoenfeld et al., "Dose-Response Relationship Between Weekly Resistance
Training Volume and Increases in Muscle Mass: A Systematic Review and Meta-Analysis,"
Journal of Sports Sciences 35, no. 11 (June 2017): 1073–82,
https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/27433992/.
```
