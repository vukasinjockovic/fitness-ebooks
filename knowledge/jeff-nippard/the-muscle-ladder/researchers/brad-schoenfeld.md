## Summary

Dr. Brad Schoenfeld is one of the most prolific researchers in exercise science, specializing in hypertrophy and resistance training. His meta-analyses and studies on volume, rep ranges, training frequency, and rest periods are frequently cited as foundational evidence in modern bodybuilding literature.

## Key Research Areas

1. **Volume-Hypertrophy Relationship** - Meta-analyses showing dose-response relationship between sets and muscle growth
2. **Rep Range Research** - Studies challenging traditional "hypertrophy rep range" myths
3. **Training Frequency** - Research on optimal training frequency per muscle group
4. **Rest Periods** - Studies on rest interval effects on hypertrophy

## Key Findings Cited

1. **Load doesn't determine hypertrophy** - 2014 study: 3-rep sets vs 10-rep sets produced similar muscle growth when volume-equated
   — Source: The Muscle Ladder, Ch.10

2. **Longer rest periods benefit growth** - 2016 study: Longer rest periods enhanced both strength and hypertrophy
   — Source: The Muscle Ladder, Ch.11

3. **Volume dose-response** - 2017 meta-analysis: Higher volumes (10+ sets/week) produce more growth than lower volumes
   — Source: The Muscle Ladder, Ch.8

4. **Frequency meta-analysis** - 2019: Training muscle 2x/week may be superior to 1x/week
   — Source: The Muscle Ladder, Ch.9
