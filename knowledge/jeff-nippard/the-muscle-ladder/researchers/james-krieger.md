# James Krieger

**Type:** researcher
**Status:** solid

## Summary

James Krieger is an exercise scientist known for his research on training volume and its relationship to hypertrophy. He runs Weightology, a website providing evidence-based fitness content, and has authored influential meta-analyses on optimal training volume for muscle growth.

## Key Contributions

1. **Volume Meta-Analysis** - Led meta-analysis showing higher volumes favor greater hypertrophy (11 of 15 studies favored higher volume)
   — Source: The Muscle Ladder, Ch.6

2. **Weightology Evidence-Based Guides** - "Set Volume for Muscle Size: The Ultimate Evidence Based Bible"
   — Source: The Muscle Ladder, References

3. **Collaborative Research** - Co-author with Schoenfeld on multiple meta-analyses

## Key Findings Cited

From the volume meta-analysis:
> "The data in Figure 6.3 is taken from a meta-analysis led by James Krieger. As you can see, eleven of the fifteen studies included in the study favored higher volumes, as does the average overall effect."
