# Eric Helms

**Type:** researcher
**Status:** solid
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-03
**Aliases:** Dr. Eric Helms

## Summary

Dr. Eric Helms is an exercise scientist, natural bodybuilder, and author whose work on RPE (Rating of Perceived Exertion) and evidence-based training has been highly influential in the fitness community. His "Muscle & Strength Pyramid" books directly inspired Jeff Nippard's Muscle Ladder framework.

## Key Contributions

1. **RPE Research** - Doctoral dissertation on RPE for resistance training; continues research with bodybuilders and powerlifters
   — Source: The Muscle Ladder, Ch.6

2. **Muscle & Strength Pyramid** - Book series organizing training principles hierarchically
   — Source: The Muscle Ladder, Acknowledgments

3. **Evidence-Based Coaching** - Bridges gap between research and practical application
