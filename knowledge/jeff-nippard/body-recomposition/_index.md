# The Ultimate Guide to Body Recomposition

**Authors:** Jeff Nippard, BS & Chris Barakat, MS, ATC, CISSN
**Year:** 2019
**Publisher:** CRC Press / Taylor and Francis Group
**ISBN:** 9781032095998

## Overview

A comprehensive guide to simultaneous fat loss and muscle gain (body recomposition). Unlike traditional bulking/cutting cycles, this book teaches optimization strategies for achieving both goals concurrently through attention to nutrition, training, sleep, and supplementation.

## Core Thesis

Body recomposition requires more attention to detail than simple bulking or cutting. While weight loss = calories in < calories out, and weight gain = calories in > calories out, body recomposition requires optimizing multiple variables simultaneously:
1. Calorie intake (based on goal, body composition, training experience)
2. Macronutrient distribution (especially protein)
3. Nutrient timing
4. Training stimulus (progressive overload)
5. Recovery (sleep, stress management)

## Key Quote

> "There are no shortcuts to any place worth going... when we say optimize, we mean that we are valuing the most effective approach over the most time-efficient approach."
