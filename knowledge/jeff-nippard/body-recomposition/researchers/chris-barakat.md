## Summary

Chris Barakat is a sports scientist, certified athletic trainer, and sports nutritionist who co-authored The Ultimate Guide to Body Recomposition with Jeff Nippard. He holds an MS degree and certifications as an Athletic Trainer (ATC) and Certified Sports Nutritionist (CISSN). Known for bringing academic rigor to practical fitness applications.
