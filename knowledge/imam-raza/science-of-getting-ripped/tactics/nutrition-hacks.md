# 18 Nutrition Hacks

**Type:** tactic
**Status:** solid
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-04
**Aliases:** Nutrition tips, diet hacks, eating guidelines

## Summary

A practical, lifestyle-focused approach to nutrition for busy people who can't follow strict meal plans. These 18 hacks emphasize simple principles over complex diets, acknowledging that real life includes family gatherings, business lunches, and social events. Target: 40% carbs, 30% protein, 30% fat.

## Philosophy

> "Give me a one-page bullet-list of exactly what I should do. That's worth more to me than a stack of books that I have to dig through to get to the good stuff." — Alwyn Cosgrove

## The 18 Hacks

### 1. Eliminate All Drinks Except Water
> "Gatorade, sports drinks, cola's, alcohol, etc. add unnecessary calories to your diet. You should be drinking at least 2 liters of water per day."

**Tip:** Add lemon for flavor and detoxification.

### 2. Eliminate Sugar and Starchy Carbs
> "Simply removing donuts, cupcakes, chips, cookies, candy bars... will have a tremendous impact in helping you get in shape. You'll likely drop a few pounds in a few weeks just by making these tweaks."

**Key:** Eat one-ingredient foods (almonds, berries, eggs, meat, fish, vegetables).

### 3. Eat Lots of Vegetables
> "Vegetables have lots of fiber which is great for your digestive system, lowering cholesterol, etc. Also, they have anti-oxidants that help fight cancer... they keep you fuller longer."

**Tip:** Sauté in garlic and a little salt.

### 4. Eat Enough Protein
> "High-protein diets not only promote muscle hypertrophy (growth) but they also enhance fat loss... more protein boosts levels of peptide YY, a hormone that decreases hunger and increases satiety."

### 5. Avoid Refined Carbs
> "Choose slow-digesting whole grains such as brown rice, oatmeal and whole-wheat bread, which keep insulin levels low and steady."

**Research:** Penn State study showed whole grain dieters lost more abdominal fat.

### 6. Fat is Important
> "Some fats, like omega-3's can help promote fat loss... If you strip fat from your diet, you'll sabotage your weight loss goals because you'll always feel unsatisfied."

**Sources:** Salmon, sardines, olive oil, peanut butter, walnuts.

### 7. Eat Whole Eggs
> "Eggs provide high amounts of protein and cholesterol. Cholesterol is a key building block of testosterone... research shows that people consuming eggs for breakfast ate fewer calories throughout the rest of the day."

### 8. Eat More Grapefruit
> "Scripps Clinic research showed subjects who ate half a grapefruit three times a day lost approximately 4 pounds in 12 weeks... likely due to grapefruit's ability to lower insulin levels."

### 9. Use Spicy Condiments
> "Cholula, Louisiana hot sauce, crushed red peppers, chili ginger paste... not only adds tons of flavor, it helps promote better calorie burning. The effects are enhanced when you consume caffeine."

### 10. Eat Avocados
> "Avocados have monounsaturated fat, which isn't typically stored as body fat. They also contain mannoheptulose, that blunts insulin release and enhances calcium absorption."

### 11. Drink Green Tea
> "The main ingredient, EGCG, helps inhibit the enzyme that breaks down norepinephrine. Norepinephrine helps keep the metabolic rate up."

**Research:** Journal of Nutrition showed green tea + exercise lost more abdominal fat.

### 12. Drink Ice Cold Water
> "German researchers discovered that drinking about 2 cups of ice cold water temporarily boosts metabolic rate by roughly 30%... due to an increase in norepinephrine."

### 13. Avoid Artificial Sweeteners
> "Beverages like diet soda mess with your brain's ability to regulate calorie intake, causing you to feel hungrier than normal so you eat more total calories."

### 14. Be Smart About Portions
> "Cornell University reported that moviegoers given a large container of popcorn ate 45% more than those given a medium container."

**Application:** Large servings for protein, small for starches.

### 15. Use an App
> "Use an app like FitDay.com or MyFitnessPal.com to give you a good idea of how much you're eating."

### 16. More Carbs on Lifting Days
> "Eat more carbs on your weight lifting days, preferably AFTER lifting weights to replenish your glycogen stores."

**Good carbs:** Sweet potatoes, russet potatoes, jasmine rice.

### 17. Combine Protein and Carbs
> "Eating potatoes and protein will help lessen the insulin spike that the carbs would trigger if eaten alone."

### 18. You Don't Have to Be Perfect
> "If you eat right 85% to 95% of the time, you'll see great results."
