# The Science of Getting Ripped

**Author:** Raza Imam
**Year:** 2016
**Publisher:** Self-published

## Overview

A practical, "hacks-oriented" guide to fat loss and muscle building aimed at busy professionals. Presents 11 training principles and 18 nutrition tips in an accessible format. Less academic than other fitness books but provides actionable frameworks for time-constrained individuals.

## Core Thesis

Getting ripped requires two things:
1. Losing body fat (need 6-12% to see abs)
2. Building/maintaining muscle mass

The book emphasizes that "everything works" (all training methods have merit), but "nothing works forever" (body adapts, requiring variation). Fitness is "intensity dependent, not time dependent" - making it suitable for busy people who can only train 3x/week for 30-40 minutes.

## Three Secrets of the Fitness Industry

1. **Everything works** - All training methods have merit; choose based on your goals
2. **Nothing works forever** - Body adapts; must vary routine
3. **Most workouts are the same** - Basic principles apply across methods
