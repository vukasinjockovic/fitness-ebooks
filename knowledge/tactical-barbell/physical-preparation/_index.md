# Tactical Barbell: Physical Preparation for Law Enforcement (2015)
**Author:** K. Black
**Published:** 2015
**Series:** Tactical Barbell

## Overview
A 12-week structured training program designed to prepare candidates for law enforcement physical fitness tests and police academy training. Emphasizes building a foundation of general strength and conditioning before transitioning to police-specific physical preparation.

## Core Philosophy
- Consistency over intensity
- Build general foundation before specificity
- Strength and conditioning are equally important
- Simple, repeatable programming

## Key Quote
> "It's not about going 110% every single training session... It's about perseverance and CONSISTENCY. Simply complete what you've been assigned."
