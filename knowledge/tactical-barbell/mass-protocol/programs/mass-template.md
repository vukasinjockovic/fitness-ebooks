# Mass Template (MT)
**Type:** program
**Status:** solid
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-03
**Source:** Tactical Barbell: Mass Protocol (2018) by K. Black
**Aliases:** MT, Mass Template

## Summary
Mass Template is the flagship hypertrophy program in Mass Protocol. It's an unconventional 4-day template that uses high frequency to maximize muscle growth while concurrently developing maximal strength. It's a "distant forefather" to the popular Operator template.

## Philosophy
> "Drop any preconceived notions you may have of what a hypertrophy program should look like. Mass Template is a powerful and unconventional approach to growing muscle."
— Source: "Mass Template" chapter

## Exercise Cluster
Only 4 exercises:
1. **Bench Press** - 3x/week
2. **Squat** - 3x/week
3. **Weighted Pull-ups** - 3x/week
4. **Deadlift** - 1x/week (dedicated day)
— Source: "Mass Template" chapter

## Weekly Schedule
| Day | Exercises |
|-----|-----------|
| Day 1 | Bench, Squat, WPU |
| Day 2 | Off |
| Day 3 | Bench, Squat, WPU |
| Day 4 | Off |
| Day 5 | Bench, Squat, WPU |
| Day 6 | Deadlift only |
| Day 7 | Off |

## Why Weighted Pull-ups?
> "WPUs sufficiently cover off your biceps and arms to the point that you don't have to train them separately. Deadlifts alone don't do that to the same degree."
— Source: "Mass Template" chapter

Additional benefits:
- Easy transition to military/police PFTs
- Extraordinary back/lat/grip development
- "You will grow wings over time"
— Source: "Mass Template" chapter

## Execution Guidelines
- Rest 2-3 minutes between sets (5+ if needed)
- Objective: Complete all reps while minimizing failure
- Fatigue on last reps acceptable, complete failure = increase rest or lower weight

## Intensity Techniques
- **AMRAP** - As Many Reps As Possible on final set (Week 3)
- **Peaking** - Work up to heavy triples/doubles/singles
— Source: "Mass Template" chapter
