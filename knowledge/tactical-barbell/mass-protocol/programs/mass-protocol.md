# Mass Protocol
**Type:** program
**Status:** solid
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-03
**Source:** Tactical Barbell: Mass Protocol (2018) by K. Black
**Aliases:** MP, Tactical Barbell Mass

## Summary
Mass Protocol is a comprehensive hypertrophy program designed for tactical professionals who need to build functional muscle mass while maintaining operational readiness. It balances muscle growth with conditioning to create "useful" mass rather than purely aesthetic development.

## Target Audience
- Patrol officers needing physical presence
- Corrections officers
- Firefighters offsetting heavy gear
- Undercover operatives developing a specific look
- Any tactical professional where mass is beneficial
— Source: "Introduction" chapter

## Program Structure

### Phase 1: Base Building (Optional, 6 weeks)
- Strength-Endurance training 3x/week
- Endurance training 3x/week
- Prepares joints, connective tissue, and aerobic system
— Source: "Base Building" section

### Phase 2: General Mass (Variable length)
Four template options:
1. **Mass Template** - 4-day frequency-based
2. **Grey Man** - 3-day A/B alternating
3. **Gladiator** - 4-day with AMRAP/peaking
4. **Fighter HT** - Minimalist 3-day

### Phase 3: Specificity (3-week blocks)
Fine-tuning with isolation work:
- **Alpha** - 50/50 strength/hypertrophy split
- **Bravo** - Pure hypertrophy focus
— Source: "Specificity" section

## The Holy Trinity Foundation
All training built on three pillars:
1. Resistance Training (stimulus)
2. Nutrition (building material)
3. Conditioning (support system)
— Source: "The Holy Trinity" chapter

## Key Principles

### Frequency Over Volume
Uses moderate volume spread across more sessions rather than one high-volume session per muscle group.

### Performance-Based Mass
> "Our programs are designed with the tactical professional in mind. Performance is always on the table."
— Source: "Overview" chapter

### Concurrent Development
Strength and hypertrophy developed simultaneously during General Mass phase.
