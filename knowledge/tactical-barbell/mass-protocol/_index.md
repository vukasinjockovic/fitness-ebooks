# Tactical Barbell: Mass Protocol (2018)
**Author:** K. Black
**Published:** 2018
**Series:** Tactical Barbell

## Overview
A hypertrophy-focused program designed for tactical professionals who need to build functional muscle mass while maintaining operational readiness. Unlike typical bodybuilding programs, Mass Protocol balances muscle growth with conditioning requirements.

## Core Philosophy
- The Holy Trinity: Resistance Training + Nutrition + Conditioning
- Frequency-based hypertrophy approach
- Build "useful" mass for tactical professionals
- Performance always on the table

## Key Quote
> "Think of the Trinity like a 3-digit phone number. If you're off by even one digit you're not going to get through even if all the other digits are correct."
