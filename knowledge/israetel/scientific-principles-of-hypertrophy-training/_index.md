## Overview

This book presents the 7 scientific principles that govern effective hypertrophy training, organized from most to least important. It provides a complete theoretical framework for program design based on sport science research.

## The 7 Principles (Ranked by Importance)

1. **Specificity** - Training must directly or indirectly support muscle growth
2. **Overload** - Training must be challenging and progressively harder
3. **Fatigue Management** - Planned strategies to alleviate fatigue
4. **SRA (Stimulus Recovery Adaptation)** - Optimal training frequency
5. **Variation** - Strategic change to maintain effectiveness
6. **Phase Potentiation** - Arrange phases to enhance subsequent phases
7. **Individualization** - Adapt to individual differences
