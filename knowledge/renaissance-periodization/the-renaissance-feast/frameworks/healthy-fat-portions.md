# Healthy Fat Portions Guide

**Type:** Framework
**Status:** Solid
**Source:** The Renaissance Feast, Quick Guides (p. 123)
**Author:** Lori Shaw

## Summary

A quick reference guide for measuring healthy fat portions when you need to hit specific fat macro targets. All measurements based on 1 serving = 15g fat from healthy sources.

## The Measurement Table

*All nut measurements are for RAW nuts*

| Food | 1/2 Serving (7.5g fat) | 1 Serving (15g fat) |
|------|------------------------|---------------------|
| **Almonds, whole** | 11 nuts / 0.5 oz / 14g | 22 nuts / 1 oz / 28g |
| **Almonds, chopped** | 0.5 oz / 14g | 1 oz / 28g |
| **Almond meal/flour** | 1/8 Cup | 1/4 Cup |
| **Almond butter** | 1 Tbsp | 2 Tbsp |
| **Avocado** | 1/4 avocado (~50g) | 1/2 avocado (~100g) |
| **Canola oil** | 1/2 Tbsp | 1 Tbsp |
| **Cashews** | 0.625 oz / 17.5g | 1.25 oz / 35g |
| **Cashew butter** | 1 Tbsp | 2 Tbsp |
| **Olive oil** | 1/2 Tbsp | 1 Tbsp |
| **Peanuts** | 0.5 oz / 14g | 1 oz / 28g |
| **Peanut butter** | 1 Tbsp | 2 Tbsp |
| **Pecan halves** | 7 halves / 10.5g | 14 halves / 21g |
| **Pecans, chopped** | 1.5 Tbsp / 0.375 oz | 3 Tbsp / 0.75 oz |
| **Pistachios** | 0.625 oz / 17.5g | 1.25 oz / 35g |
| **Walnut halves** | 6 halves / 0.4 oz / 11.5g | 12 halves / 0.8 oz / 23g |
| **Walnuts, chopped** | 1.5 Tbsp / 0.4 oz / 11.5g | 3 Tbsp / 0.8 oz / 23g |

## Key Distinction

> "*Grams of fat FROM healthy sources, not total weight of food - this is the number that matches your template.*"

The serving sizes are based on grams of fat content, not weight of the food itself.

## Why This Matters

When dieting with RP templates:
- Templates specify fat in grams (e.g., "15g fat per meal")
- Without a reference, measuring "15g of fat" is abstract
- This guide translates grams of fat → physical portions

## Common Patterns

### Nut Butters (Almonds, Cashews, Peanuts)
- 1 Tbsp = approximately 7-8g fat (half serving)
- 2 Tbsp = approximately 15g fat (full serving)

### Oils (Olive, Canola, etc.)
- 1/2 Tbsp = approximately 7.5g fat
- 1 Tbsp = approximately 15g fat

### Whole Nuts
- Portion sizes vary by nut type
- Pecans and walnuts: fewer pieces per serving
- Almonds and cashews: more pieces per serving

## Practical Applications

### Meal Prep
Know exactly how much fat source to add to each container.

### Recipe Scaling
Adjust fat in recipes to hit your specific targets.

### Quick Reference
Post this on your refrigerator for immediate access.

### Grocery Shopping
Know what quantities to buy for the week.
