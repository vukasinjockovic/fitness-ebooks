# The Renaissance Feast - Book Index

**Full Title:** The Renaissance Feast: 101 Recipes (Recipes 101-200)
**Author:** Lori Shaw
**Publisher:** Renaissance Periodization
**Year:** 2017 (Copyright)
**Series:** Renaissance Periodization Book 14

## Overview

The second RP cookbook, sequel to "The Renaissance Kitchen" (recipes 1-100). Contains 100 macro-friendly recipes (numbered 101-200) with full nutritional information per ingredient and per serving. Each recipe includes a "Your Changes" column for customizing portions to individual macro requirements.

## Key Features

1. **Macro Transparency** - Every recipe lists protein, net carbs, and fat for each ingredient
2. **Flexible Portioning** - Multiple serving size options with pre-calculated macros
3. **Customization Column** - Space to adjust ingredients for your specific macro needs
4. **Phase-Appropriate Labeling** - Some recipes marked as "best for Maintenance or Massing"
5. **Quick Reference Guides** - Fat and carb measurement guides in appendix

## Practical Guidance

### Nutrition Label Calculator
To calculate portion sizes for your macro needs:

1. **A** = Grams of fat (or carb/protein) you need for your meal
2. **B** = Grams of that macro in one serving (from label)
3. **C** = Weight in grams of one serving (from label)
4. **D** = A ÷ B (your multiplier)
5. **Answer** = D × C (grams of food to weigh out)

### Portioning Raw Proteins
1. Calculate number of portions based on raw protein amount before cooking
2. Proceed with recipe, then divide completed dish into that number of portions
3. If consuming entire recipe over several days, exact equality doesn't matter
4. For precision (e.g., contest prep): weigh completed dish ÷ number of servings = weight per portion

## Sample Recipe (Macro-Tracked Format)

### Eat Your Greens Chicken Stew
*Comfort food, versatile recipe*

**Ingredients:**
- 4 Tbsp EVOO, divided (60g fat)
- 5 Cups greens (kale, spinach, or blend)
- 1 Cup chopped parsley
- 1 med onion, chopped
- 1.5 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts (144g protein)
- Spices (salt, curry, cinnamon)
- 2 Tbsp tomato paste
- 4 Cups sweet potato cubes (91g carbs)
- 2 Cups chicken broth
- 3 Tbsp lemon juice

**Macros:**
| Portion | Protein | Net Carbs | Fat |
|---------|---------|-----------|-----|
| Total Recipe | 144g | 91g | 60g |
| 8 servings | 18g | 11g | 8g |
| 5 servings | 29g | 18g | 12g |
