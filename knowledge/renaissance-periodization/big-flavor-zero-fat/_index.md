# Big Flavor, Zero Fat - Book Index

**Full Title:** Big Flavor, Zero Fat: 50 Nonfat RP Recipes
**Author:** Lori Shaw
**Publisher:** Renaissance Periodization
**Year:** 2018 (Copyright)
**Series:** Renaissance Periodization Book 16

## Overview

A specialized cookbook designed for deep fat loss phases when dietary fat must be minimized. Contains 50 recipes with zero or near-zero fat content, allowing dieters to maximize food volume while staying on track with caloric restrictions.

## Purpose and Use Case

> "When we're deep into the fat loss process and all but critically-needed fats have been cut out of the diet to allow calories to drop to the needed levels to stay on track with fat burning... having the ability to create meals that are low in fat, or even completely fat free, can be the difference between a successfully executed diet and a diet which runs into serious troubles of practicality."
> — Dr. Mike Israetel, Foreword

### Primary Use Cases
1. **Deep fat loss phases** - When fat macros are at minimum
2. **Advanced muscle gain** - Lower fat, higher carb approach
3. **High-volume eating** - Maximize food bulk on restricted calories

## Key Techniques for Fat-Free Cooking

### Cooking Without Oil
- Use cooking spray instead of oil
- Cook in chicken broth/stock
- Use nonstick pans
- Employ slow cooker methods

### Fat-Free Protein Sources
- Boneless skinless chicken breast
- Lean ground beef (96% lean)
- Eye of round roast
- Egg whites
- Nonfat Greek yogurt
- Nonfat ricotta

### Creating Richness Without Fat
- Nonfat Greek yogurt for creaminess
- Nonfat sour cream
- Nonfat ricotta cheese
- Nutritional yeast (for cheesy flavor)
- Concentrated broths
- Flavor emulsions

### Breading Techniques
- Egg white wash (not whole eggs)
- Whole grain cereal flakes
- Pretzels
- Whole grain breadcrumbs

## Alternative Sweeteners
From Introduction:
- **Date sugar** - Made entirely from ground dates, lower carbs than sugar, earthier flavor
- **Beet sugar** - Use sparingly
- **Coconut sugar** - Debatable benefit, use sparingly
- **Brown rice syrup** - Clean carb source
- **SF syrups** - Sugar-free options for zero-carb sweetening

## Sample Recipe Examples

### Chicken Zoodle Soup (Recipe #3)
**Macros:** 96g protein total / 0 carbs / 0 fat
*Perfect for deep cut - pure protein*

**Ingredients:**
- 3 chopped leeks
- 3 garlic cloves
- 6-8 Cups chicken broth
- 1 lb cooked chicken (96g protein)
- Fresh thyme
- Package spiralized zucchini (zoodles)

### Korean Spiced Elk (Recipe #17)
**Macros per 7 portions:** 18g protein / 1g carbs / 0g fat

**Key technique:** Touch of sweetness from SF syrup + tamari creates satisfying flavor without fat.

### Mexican Meatza (Recipe #16)
**Macros:** 111g protein / 20g carbs / 2g fat total
*Meat "crust" pizza alternative*

**Key technique:** Pat lean beef thin, bake, drain fat completely, then top.
