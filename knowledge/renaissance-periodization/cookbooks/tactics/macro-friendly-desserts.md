## Phase Appropriateness

### Everyday Eating (Cutting OK)
- Recipes with minimal added sugar
- Nonfat/lowfat dairy based
- Protein powder desserts
- Date sugar sweetened items

### Special Occasions Only
- Recipes with coconut oil
- Beet sugar/coconut sugar items
- Multi-layer cakes
- High-fat items (pecan pies, cookie cakes)

### Maintenance/Massing Treats
- Chocolate splurges
- Full-fat versions
- Higher carb desserts

## Complete Meal Desserts

### Raspberry Lemon Cheesecake
**Per serving (7 portions)**:
- 17g protein
- 32g carbs
- 7g fat

Key technique: Nonfat ricotta + Greek yogurt base, no crust

### Nonfat Pumpkin Roll
**Per slice (22 slices)**:
- 4g protein
- 10g carbs
- 0g fat

Key technique: Oat-based dough, protein powder filling

### 2-Minute Chocolate Cake for 1
**Total**:
- 1g protein
- 16g carbs
- 16g fat (8g without PB topping)

Key technique: Microwave mug cake with oats

## Fat Reduction Strategies

### Substitution Options
| High Fat | Lower Fat |
|----------|-----------|
| Whole eggs | Egg whites only |
| Oil | Unsweetened applesauce |
| Peanut butter | Powdered PB |
| Coconut oil | Cooking spray |
| Full-fat dairy | Nonfat Greek yogurt |
| Nuts | Leave off or reduce |

### Example Modifications
> "If your goal is lower fat, play around with subbing all oil for applesauce, using only egg whites and powdered peanut butter instead of the real thing."
> — Lori Shaw

## Sweetener Strategy

### Zero Carb Options
- SF syrups (Torani)
- SF pancake syrup
- Monkfruit sweetener (Lakanto)
- Flavor emulsions

### RP-Approved Carb Sweeteners
- Brown rice syrup (240g carbs/cup)
- Date sugar (lower than regular sugar)

### Special Occasion Only
- Beet sugar
- Coconut sugar

## Protein Integration

### Protein Powder Uses
- Frosting base: Protein powder + Greek yogurt
- Dough: Casein + oats
- Smoothie bowls
- Puddings

### Casein Benefits
- Thicker texture than whey
- Better for "dough" recipes
- Slower digesting (satiety)

## Practical Tips

### Portion Control
- Pre-portion into individual servings
- Use silicone molds for mini versions
- "Pie-lets" instead of full pies
- Single-serve mug cakes

### Make-Ahead Strategies
- Freeze baked goods immediately (keeps moist)
- Defrost portions as needed
- Prepare fillings separately
- Batch prep on weekends

### Holiday/Special Occasion
> "I wrote this cookbook... so that you don't feel like you have to lose control over your diet during what should be fun, relaxed and happy times with family and friends!"

Know your macros → maintain control → enjoy without guilt
