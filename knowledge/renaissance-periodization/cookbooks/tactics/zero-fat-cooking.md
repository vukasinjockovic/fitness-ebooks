# Zero-Fat Cooking Strategies

**Source**: RP 16 - Big Flavor, Zero Fat (2018) by Lori Shaw
**Purpose**: Techniques for fat loss phases when fats are at minimum

## When to Use Zero-Fat Cooking

### Fat Loss Context
> "When we're deep into the fat loss process and all but critically-needed fats have been cut out of the diet to allow calories to drop to the needed levels to stay on track with fat burning."
> — Dr. Mike Israetel, Foreword

### Also Useful For
- Advanced athletes in higher-carb massing phases
- Post-workout meals (protein + carbs, minimal fat)
- When fat allotment is saved for other meals

## Core Techniques

### Protein Cooking
1. **Dry heat methods**
   - Air frying
   - Baking/roasting
   - Grilling
   - Broiling

2. **Wet heat methods**
   - Slow cooker (no added fat)
   - Cooking in broth
   - Steaming

3. **Pan cooking**
   - Nonstick skillets with cooking spray
   - Blot grease with paper towels held by tongs

### Fat Removal
- Drain cooked ground meat thoroughly
- "Tilt pan, use paper towels held with tongs to get rid of excess grease"
- Let excess liquid drain from cooked vegetables
- For meatza: "Let hot grease run off, then blot with paper towels"

### Breading Without Fat
- Egg white wash (not whole eggs)
- Crushed whole grain cereals (spelt flakes, pretzels)
- Greek yogurt coating
- Process: Dip in egg whites → coat in crumbs → air fry/bake

## Flavor Building Without Fat

### Umami Sources
- Worcestershire sauce
- Tamari (wheat-free soy sauce)
- Fish sauce
- Nutritional yeast
- Anchovy paste
- Sundried tomatoes (dry-packed, not oil-packed)

### Sweet Elements (Zero Carb)
- SF syrups (caramel, peanut butter, vanilla)

- Brown sugar cinnamon SF syrup

## Sample Meal Macros

### Chicken Zoodle Soup
- **Total**: 96g protein, 0 carbs, 0 fat
- **Per serving (3)**: 32g P / 0g C / 0g F
- Perfect for deep cut protein meal

### Middle Eastern Slow Cooker Chicken
- **Total**: 263g protein, 18g carbs, 0 fat
- **Per serving (9)**: 29g P / 2g C / 0g F
- Batch cooking friendly

### Skillet Burger
- **Total**: 96g protein, 6g carbs, 0 fat
- **Per serving (3)**: 32g P / 2g C / 0g F
- All the burger flavor, no bun needed

## Practical Tips

### Meal Prep Efficiency
- Batch cook proteins on weekends
- Use slow cooker for hands-off cooking
- Pre-portion into containers
- Label with macros

### Making Zero-Fat Satisfying
> "Giant Bowl of Sausage Pasta for One... is a GIANT bowl of food that really sticks with me. And, you know how valuable that sensation is when you're cutting!"
> — Lori Shaw

### Volume Strategy
- Add extra vegetables (unlimited)
- Use riced cauliflower to bulk up dishes
- Zoodles instead of pasta
- Large salad bases

### Keeping it Interesting
- Rotate cuisines (Mexican, Korean, Middle Eastern)
- Change cooking methods
- Experiment with spice blends
- "Small, easy, quick changes can really pay off in the flavor dept when you're dieting!"
