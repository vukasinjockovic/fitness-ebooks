# RP Cookbook Ingredient Guide

**Source**: RP Cookbooks (14, 16, 18) by Lori Shaw
**Purpose**: RP-approved and phase-specific ingredient alternatives

## RP-Approved Sweeteners

### Fully Approved (Cutting/All Phases)
| Ingredient | Description | Use For |
|------------|-------------|---------|
| Brown rice syrup | 100% brown rice, mild sweetener | Replaces honey |
| SF (sugar-free) syrups | Torani, Jordan's Skinny | Flavor without carbs |
| SF pancake syrup | Standard grocery store brands | Breakfast recipes |
| Date sugar | Ground dates, lower carbs than sugar | Recipes with earthy flavor |
| Monkfruit (Lakanto) | White/brown/liquid forms | Sugar replacement |

### Use Sparingly (Maintenance/Massing Only)
| Ingredient | Notes | Phase |
|------------|-------|-------|
| Beet sugar | Debatable vs cane sugar | Massing/Maintenance |
| Coconut sugar | Debatable vs cane sugar | Massing/Maintenance |
| Coconut oil | High saturated fat, not for frequent use | Special occasions |

### Avoid
- Butter
- Cane sugar
- Straight stevia (taste issues)
- White flour
- Honey

## RP-Approved Flours

| Flour | Carbs/Cup | Best For |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| Whole grain pastry flour | ~76g | Cakes, tartlets |
| Spelt flour | ~76g | Baked goods |
| Sprouted wheat flour | ~96g | Crusts, crisps |

## Lean Protein Sources

### Extra Lean Beef Cuts (>20 cuts qualify)
1. Eye of round roast or steak
2. Sirloin tip side steak
3. Top round roast and steak
4. Bottom round roast and steak
5. Top sirloin steak

### Zero-Fat Protein Cooking
- Use nonstick skillets with cooking spray
- Cook in chicken broth instead of oil
- Blot excess grease with paper towels
- Choose skinless poultry
- Select water-packed fish

## Dairy Substitutions

### For Cutting
| Instead of | Use | Notes |
|------------|-----|-------|
| Regular sour cream | Nonfat sour cream | |
| Regular yogurt | Nonfat Greek yogurt | Adds protein |
| Regular ricotta | Nonfat ricotta | |
| Regular cheese | 2% or part-skim | |
| Cream cheese | Greek yogurt + protein powder | "Frosting" base |

### For Fat-Free Cooking
- Nonfat Greek yogurt as creamy base
- Nonfat whipped topping for desserts
- Fat-free refried beans for Mexican dishes

## Specialty Ingredients

### Flavor Emulsions
- More concentrated than extracts
- Designed for cold/frozen dishes
- NY Cheesecake, Banana Royale, Cookie, Butter flavors
- Available online in small bottles

### Nutritional Yeast
- Deactivated yeast with nutty/cheesy flavor
- 6g protein, 2g carbs per 2 Tbsp
- Use in "meatza" and savory recipes

### Protein Powders in Cooking
- Vanilla: Frostings, puddings, smoothies
- Chocolate: Cakes, brownies, mousse
- 80/20 whey/casein blends work best for baking
- Casein: Thicker texture for "dough" recipes

## Phase-Specific Ingredient Use

### Deep Cut
Use RP 16 recipes with:
- Zero added fats
- Cooking spray only
- Nonfat dairy
- Lean proteins

### Moderate Cut
- Half-serving fats allowed
- Can include some RP 14 recipes
- Adjust portions down

### Maintenance
- Full fat servings OK
- All cookbooks applicable
- Can include "Chocolate Splurges"

### Massing
- Higher carbs OK
- Treats more frequent
- RP 18 desserts appropriate

## Cooking Tips

### For Moisture Without Fat
- Unsweetened applesauce replaces oil
- Egg whites replace whole eggs
- Greek yogurt adds moisture to baked goods
- Cooking in broth instead of oil

### For Flavor Without Carbs
- Fresh herbs (don't count macros)
- SF syrups for sweetness
- Spice blends (Mrs Dash, Five Spice)
- Citrus zest and juice
- Vinegars (balsamic, rice wine)

### Abbreviations in Recipes
- S&P = Salt and pepper
- SF = Sugar-free
- EVOO = Extra virgin olive oil
- "Clean" = Only RP-approved ingredients
